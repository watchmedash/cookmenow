Apple Crumble

Ingredients

  • 5 cups chopped peeled tart apples
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large bowl, combine apples and sugar. Transfer to a greased 2-qt. baking dish.
  2. In the same bowl, combine the oats, flour and cinnamon; add butter and mix well.
  3. Sprinkle over apple mixture. Bake until apples are tender, about 50-55 minutes. Serve warm, with ice cream if desired.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 55 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes
