Apple Pie

Ingredients

For the Pie Crust:
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup ice water
For the Apple Filling:
  • 6 cups thinly sliced and peeled apples (such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, diced

Instructions

For the Pie Crust:
  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, salt, and granulated sugar.
  2. Add the chilled and cubed unsalted butter to the flour mixture. Use a pastry cutter or your hands to cut the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Sprinkle ice water, a few tablespoons at a time, over the flour-butter mixture. Gently mix the dough until it comes together. Be careful not to overwork the dough.
  4. Divide the dough into two equal portions, flatten each into a disk, and wrap them in plastic wrap. Refrigerate the dough for at least 1 hour or overnight.
For the Apple Filling:
  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the thinly sliced and peeled apples, granulated sugar, light brown sugar, lemon juice, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, and salt. Toss the mixture until the apples are well coated.
  2. Sprinkle all-purpose flour over the apple mixture and toss again to combine.
For Assembling:
  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one portion of the chilled pie crust into a circle that fits your pie dish. Carefully transfer the crust to the dish and press it gently into the bottom and sides.
  3. Add the apple filling into the pie crust and distribute it evenly.
  4. Dot the top of the filling with diced unsalted butter.
  5. Roll out the second portion of the chilled pie crust and place it over the apple filling. Trim the excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  6. Make a few slits on the top crust to allow steam to escape during baking.
  7. Optionally, brush the top crust with milk or egg wash for a golden finish.
  8. Place the pie on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes or until the crust is golden and the apple filling is bubbly.
  9. Allow the pie to cool for at least 2 hours before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes (plus chilling time for pie crust)
  • Cooking Time: 45-50 minutes
  • Total Time: 75-80 minutes (plus chilling time)

Facts about Apple Pie

  • Apple Pie is a classic American dessert that is loved and enjoyed by people of all ages.
  • The pie crust is made from a simple mixture of flour, butter, salt, and ice water, which results in a flaky and buttery pastry.
  • It is traditional to use tart apples, such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp, for the filling to balance the sweetness and add a tangy flavor.
  • The apple filling is spiced with ground cinnamon and nutmeg, which complements the natural sweetness of the apples.
  • Apple Pie is often served warm and can be enjoyed on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.
  • It is a popular dessert during holidays like Thanksgiving and Christmas, as well as year-round for family gatherings and special occasions.
  • Apple Pie has become a symbol of American tradition and comfort food, evoking feelings of warmth and nostalgia.
  • It is believed that the first apple pie recipes were brought to America by English and Dutch settlers in the 17th century.
  • Today, Apple Pie remains one of the most beloved and iconic desserts in American cuisine.
