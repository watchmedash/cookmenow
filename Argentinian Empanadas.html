Argentinian Empanadas

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup chopped green olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1 package refrigerated pie crusts
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  2. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Stir in the paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the raisins, olives, parsley, and hard-boiled eggs.
  4. Roll out the pie crusts and cut into circles. Place a spoonful of the beef mixture on one half of each circle.
  5. Fold the other half of the circle over the filling and press the edges together to seal.
  6. Brush the empanadas with the beaten egg.
  7. Bake in a preheated 375°F (190°C) oven for 20-25 minutes, until golden brown.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 55 minutes
