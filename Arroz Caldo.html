Arroz Caldo

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 boneless chicken thighs, cut into small pieces
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, julienned
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • Ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Saut√© the garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add the fish sauce and stir well to combine.
  4. Add the glutinous rice and stir for a few minutes to toast it slightly.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer. Stir occasionally to prevent the rice from sticking to the bottom of the pot.
  6. Simmer until the rice is cooked and the soup has thickened to your liking. This could take about 20-30 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with hard-boiled eggs, chopped green onions, lemon wedges, and ground black pepper.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes
