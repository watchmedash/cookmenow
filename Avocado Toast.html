Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Optional toppings: a handful of arugula, a squeeze of lime juice, chili flakes, or a poached egg

Instructions

  1. Toast your bread slices to your liking.
  2. While the bread is toasting, cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the avocado flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork until it's as smooth or chunky as you like it.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto your toasted bread slices.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Add any additional toppings if desired, and enjoy!

Time

  • Preparation Time: 5 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 5 minutes
  • Total Time: 10 minutes
