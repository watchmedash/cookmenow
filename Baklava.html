Baklava

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces phyllo dough
  • 1 pound chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, or pistachios are best, or use a combination of them)
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup honey

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Butter the bottoms and sides of a 9x13 inch pan.
  2. Toss together cinnamon and nuts. Unroll phyllo dough. Cut the whole stack to fit the size of the pan. Cover phyllo with a dampened cloth to keep from drying out as you work. Place two sheets of dough in the pan, butter thoroughly. Repeat until you have 8 sheets layered. Sprinkle 2 - 3 tablespoons of nut mixture on top. Top with two sheets of dough, butter, nuts, layering as you go. The top layer should be about 6 - 8 sheets deep.
  3. Using a sharp knife, cut into diamond or square shapes all the way to the bottom of the pan. Bake for about 50 minutes until baklava is golden and crisp.
  4. While baklava is baking, combine sugar and water in a saucepan over medium heat and bring to a boil. Stir in honey, vanilla, and reduce heat. Simmer for about 20 minutes.
  5. Remove baklava from the oven and immediately spoon sauce over it. Let it cool. Leave it uncovered as it gets soggy if wrapped up.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 70 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
