Banitsa

Ingredients

  • 1 package phyllo dough
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 lb feta cheese
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 cup plain yogurt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C) and lightly grease a baking dish.
  2. Beat 4 eggs in a bowl. Crumble the feta cheese into the beaten eggs and stir in the yogurt until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Lay a sheet of phyllo dough in the baking dish and brush it with melted butter. Repeat this with half the phyllo sheets, brushing each one with butter, laying them one at a time.
  4. Spread the cheese mixture over the layered phyllo dough. Cover this with the rest of the phyllo dough, brushing each sheet with melted butter.
  5. Beat the remaining egg in a small bowl and brush this over the top layer of phyllo dough.
  6. Bake the banitsa in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, then increase the heat to 425째F (220째C) and bake for another 10 minutes, or until the pastry is puffed and golden brown.
  7. Allow the banitsa to cool for 30 minutes before slicing and serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes
