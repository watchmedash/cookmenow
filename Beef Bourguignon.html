Beef Bourguignon

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef chuck, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons fresh thyme leaves or 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 8 ounces pearl onions, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Season the beef with salt and pepper. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Brown the beef on all sides and remove from the pot.
  2. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and garlic to the pot and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste, thyme, and bay leaf, followed by the wine and beef broth. Return the beef to the pot, bring to a simmer, then cover and cook on low for 2 hours, or until beef is tender.
  4. In a skillet, melt the butter and sauté the mushrooms and pearl onions until golden brown.
  5. In a small bowl, mix the flour with 2 tablespoons of the stew liquid to make a smooth paste. Stir into the pot and simmer for another 10-15 minutes, or until slightly thickened.
  6. Add the sautéed mushrooms and onions to the pot and cook for another 5 minutes, or until heated through.
  7. Check for seasoning and adjust with salt and pepper if needed.
  8. Remove the bay leaf, garnish with chopped parsley, and serve over mashed potatoes or noodles.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 3 hours

Enjoy your Beef Bourguignon!
