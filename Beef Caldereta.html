Filipino Beef Caldereta

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef chuck, cut into cubes
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons liver spread
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, grated
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 2 medium-sized carrots, sliced
  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, sliced
  • 1/2 cup green olives
  • 2 chili peppers
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil and saut√© the garlic and onion.
  2. Add the beef cubes to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, liver spread, and beef broth. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer until the beef is tender (about 1.5 to 2 hours). You can add more beef broth or water if needed.
  5. Once the beef is tender, add the potatoes, carrots, bell peppers, olives, and chili peppers. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Stir in the grated cheese until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 2 hours 50 minutes
