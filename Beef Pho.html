Beef Pho

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 4 inches fresh ginger root
  • 1.5 pounds oxtail
  • 1 pound beef soup bones
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
  • 4 whole star anise
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 4 quarts water
  • 1.5 pounds beef top sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 0.5 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 pound dried rice noodles
  • 0.5 cup chopped green onion
  • 1.5 cups bean sprouts
  • 1 bunch Thai basil
  • 1 lime, cut into 4 wedges
  • 0.25 cup hoisin sauce
  • 0.25 cup sriracha sauce

Instructions

  1. Char onion and ginger over an open flame (grill or gas stove). Let them cool, then rub to get the charred skin off.
  2. Place oxtail, beef soup bones, charred onion and ginger, along with cinnamon stick, peppercorns, star anise, and cloves, into a large pot. Cover with 4 quarts of water and bring to a simmer. Simmer on low for 6 hours.
  3. Strain the broth into a saucepan and set the beef tail aside. Keep the broth warm over low heat.
  4. Soak the rice noodles in hot water for about 10 minutes, then drain. Place noodles in individual serving bowls.
  5. Slice the beef sirloin thinly and place slices on top of the noodles. Ladle hot broth over noodles and beef. The heat of the broth will cook the beef.
  6. Garnish with cilantro, green onion, bean sprouts, Thai basil, and lime wedges. Add hoisin sauce and sriracha sauce to taste.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 1 hour
  • Cooking Time: 6 hours
  • Total Time: 7 hours
