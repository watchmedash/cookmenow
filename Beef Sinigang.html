Beef Sinigang

Ingredients

  • 500g beef shank, cut into chunks
  • 2 liters water
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish, peeled and sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch of kangkong (water spinach), trimmed
  • 1 packet sinigang mix (tamarind soup base)
  • Fish sauce or salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring the water to a boil. Add the beef chunks and simmer for about 1 to 1.5 hours or until the beef is tender. Skim off any foam that forms on the surface.
  2. Add the onions and tomatoes to the pot. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the radish and eggplant. Cook for about 3 to 5 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the sinigang mix and stir until it's completely dissolved in the broth. Taste and adjust the sourness by adding more sinigang mix if desired.
  5. Season with fish sauce or salt and ground black pepper to taste.
  6. Add the kangkong (water spinach) and cook for a minute or until wilted.
  7. Turn off the heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  8. Serve the Beef Sinigang hot with steamed rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 50 minutes

Enjoy this comforting and sour-savory Beef Sinigang, a classic Filipino soup that's perfect for any time of the year!
