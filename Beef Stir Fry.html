Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 200g of thinly sliced beef
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, sliced
  • 100g of broccoli
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch mixed with 1 tablespoon of water

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add garlic and stir until fragrant.
  2. Add beef and stir-fry until it's browned.
  3. Add bell pepper, carrot, and broccoli, stir-fry for a couple of minutes.
  4. Combine soy sauce and oyster sauce then pour it into the pan.
  5. Stir in the cornstarch mixture and continue to cook until the sauce has thickened.
  6. Remove from heat and serve with rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
