Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb sirloin steak, cut into thin strips
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish
  • Egg noodles or rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Season the steak strips with salt and pepper, then set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the steak strips and cook until browned. Remove the steak from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the onion, mushrooms, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are softened. Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir to combine. Cook for an additional minute.
  4. Slowly add the beef broth to the skillet, stirring constantly. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Stir in the sour cream and Dijon mustard until well combined. Return the steak to the skillet and let it simmer in the sauce for an additional 5 minutes, or until the steak is cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  6. Serve the beef stroganoff over egg noodles or rice, garnished with fresh parsley.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes

Enjoy your Beef Stroganoff!
