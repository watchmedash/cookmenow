Beef Wellington

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef tenderloin
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 500g mixed mushrooms, chopped
  • 1 large sprig fresh thyme, leaves picked
  • 12 slices prosciutto
  • English mustard, for brushing
  • 500g puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C. Season the beef with salt and pepper, then heat the olive oil in a pan and sear the beef on all sides. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add the mushrooms and thyme leaves and cook until the mushrooms release their liquid and it evaporates, leaving you with a mushroom paste, known as a duxelle.
  3. Lay out the slices of prosciutto on a sheet of plastic wrap, slightly overlapping. Spread the duxelle over the prosciutto, then place the beef on top. Roll the prosciutto around the beef using the plastic wrap to help you, then chill in the fridge for 15 minutes.
  4. Roll out the puff pastry on a floured surface to a size that will wrap around your beef. Unwrap the beef from the plastic wrap, brush it with English mustard, then place it in the middle of the pastry. Fold the pastry over the beef and seal it with a little beaten egg.
  5. Brush the pastry all over with more beaten egg, then bake in the preheated oven for about 25 minutes for medium rare beef. Allow to rest for 10 minutes before slicing.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 60 minutes

Enjoy your Beef Wellington!
