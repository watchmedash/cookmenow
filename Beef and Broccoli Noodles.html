Beef and Broccoli Noodles

Ingredients

  • 8 oz wide rice noodles
  • 1 lb flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 head broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, oyster sauce, and hoisin sauce to make the sauce. Set aside.
  3. In a large skillet or wok, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Add sliced flank steak and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until it's browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add minced garlic and grated ginger. Stir-fry for a minute until fragrant.
  6. Add broccoli florets and sliced red bell pepper. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  7. Return the beef to the skillet and pour the sauce over everything. Toss to combine and let the sauce coat the beef and vegetables.
  8. Add the cooked rice noodles to the skillet and toss to combine with the beef and vegetables.
  9. Drizzle sesame oil over the noodles and toss again to incorporate.
  10. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds, if desired.
  11. Serve the Beef and Broccoli Noodles hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes

Enjoy this savory and satisfying Beef and Broccoli Noodles for a quick and flavorful meal!
