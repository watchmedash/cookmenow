Bibimbap

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 100g beef, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, julienned
  • 100g bean sprouts
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pan, stir-fry the beef with 1 tablespoon of sesame oil and soy sauce until fully cooked. Set aside.
  2. Using the same pan, stir-fry each vegetable separately with a bit of sesame oil and a pinch of salt. Set aside.
  3. Fry the eggs sunny-side up in a small pan.
  4. Assemble the Bibimbap by placing a cup of rice in the middle of each bowl. Arrange the cooked beef and vegetables on top of the rice, and place the fried egg in the center.
  5. Serve with gochujang on the side. Mix all the ingredients together before eating, adding gochujang to taste.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes
