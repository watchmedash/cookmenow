Bibingka

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup fresh milk
  • 1 piece salted egg, sliced
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius).
  2. In a bowl, combine the rice flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs then add the sugar, coconut milk, melted butter, and fresh milk.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the egg mixture. Mix until the batter is smooth.
  5. Transfer the batter to a baking pan lined with banana leaves.
  6. Arrange the salted egg slices on top of the batter and then bake for 20 minutes.
  7. After 20 minutes, take out the bibingka from the oven. Spread butter on top and sprinkle with sugar. Top with grated cheese and grated coconut.
  8. Bake for 10 to 15 more minutes or until the top turns golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool. Serve and enjoy!

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 35 minutes
  • Total Time: 55 minutes
