Bicol Express

Ingredients

  • 500g pork belly, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons shrimp paste
  • 6 green chili peppers, sliced
  • 3 bird's eye chili (optional, adjust to your preference)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the pork belly pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Mix in the shrimp paste and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add the coconut milk, bring to a boil then lower the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes or until the pork is tender and the coconut milk is reduced.
  5. Add the sliced chili peppers and cook for another 10 minutes.
  6. Pour in the coconut cream and simmer until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour
