Black Forest Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 jar (24 oz) Morello cherries, drained and juice reserved

For the Whipped Cream:

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, and beat until smooth. Stir in boiling water until well combined (batter will be thin).
  4. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on a wire rack.
  6. For the whipped cream, beat the heavy whipping cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  7. Cut each cake layer in half horizontally. Place one layer on a serving platter and spread with whipped cream, then top with cherries. Repeat with remaining layers.
  8. Frost the top and sides of the cake with remaining whipped cream. Optionally, garnish with chocolate shavings and additional cherries.
  9. Refrigerate for at least an hour before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 25 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 35 minutes
  • Total Time: 60 minutes (plus chilling time)

Enjoy your Black Forest Cake!
