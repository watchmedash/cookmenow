Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt. In another bowl, whisk together buttermilk, milk, egg and melted butter.
  2. Pour the wet ingredients over the dry ingredients and gently whisk them together, mixing just until combined. Fold in blueberries.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with butter. For each pancake, drop 1/4 cup of batter onto skillet. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with maple syrup, if desired.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
