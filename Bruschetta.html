Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cut the baguette into slices, about 1/2-inch thick.
  3. Arrange the bread in a single layer on a baking sheet. Toast in the oven for about 10 minutes, or until crisp and light golden brown. Be sure to watch them carefully, as they can easily burn.
  4. While the bread toasts, peel and finely dice the garlic. Chop the tomatoes and basil, then combine in a bowl with the garlic. Drizzle the olive oil over the mixture, then add salt and pepper. Stir well.
  5. Once the bread is toasted, remove it from the oven. Let it cool slightly, then top each slice with the tomato mixture.
  6. Drizzle a little more olive oil over the bruschetta if desired, then serve immediately.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes

Enjoy your Bruschetta!
