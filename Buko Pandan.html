Buko Pandan

Ingredients

  • 2 cups young coconut (buko), shredded
  • 2 cups pandan water (boiled water with pandan leaves)
  • 2 packs green gelatin powder (preferably pandan-flavored)
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup small tapioca pearls, cooked
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Dissolve the gelatin powder in pandan water and bring to a boil. Stir until the gelatin powder is completely dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into a mold and let it cool. Once cooled, refrigerate for 2 hours or until completely set. Cut the set gelatin into cubes.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the condensed milk, evaporated milk, and cream. Mix well.
  4. Add the shredded young coconut, cubed gelatin, and cooked tapioca pearls to the milk mixture. Mix until all ingredients are well combined.
  5. Refrigerate for another hour before serving. Serve chilled with ice cubes.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Chilling Time: 3 hours
  • Total Time: 3 hours 25 minutes

Enjoy your Buko Pandan!
