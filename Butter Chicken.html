Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 teaspoons ginger garlic paste
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnishing

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, turmeric powder, chili powder, garam masala, ginger garlic paste and salt. Keep it aside for at least an hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add the marinated chicken to it. Cook until it's fully done and then set it aside.
  3. In the same pan, add butter and onions. Cook until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add the ginger garlic paste and stir for a few minutes. Then add the tomato puree, chili powder, garam masala and salt. Cook until oil starts separating from the masala.
  5. Add the cooked chicken and mix well. Let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the heavy cream and mix well. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour

Enjoy your Butter Chicken!
