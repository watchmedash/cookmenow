Cajun Jambalaya

Ingredients

  • 1 pound andouille sausage, sliced
  • 1 pound chicken breast, diced
  • 1 pound raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups long-grain rice
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14 oz)
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 green onions, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sausage and chicken and cook until browned. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Add the rice, diced tomatoes, Cajun seasoning, thyme, cayenne, and chicken broth. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  4. Add the sausage, chicken, bay leaves, and shrimp. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 25-30 minutes, until the rice is cooked and the shrimp is pink.
  5. Remove the bay leaves, stir in the green onions, and serve.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 50 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes
