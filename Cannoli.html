Cannoli

Ingredients

  • For the shells:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons cold unsalted butter, cut into bits
  • 2 tablespoons sweet Marsala wine
  • 1 large egg white, lightly beaten
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • For the filling:
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup mini semisweet chocolate chips
  • Zest of 1 small orange

Instructions

  1. For the shells, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and butter in a food processor. Process until the mixture resembles coarse meal.
  2. Add the Marsala and process until the dough comes together.
  3. On a lightly floured surface, knead the dough until smooth. Wrap in plastic and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough to a 1/8-inch thickness. Cut out 4-inch circles and loosely wrap each circle around a 1-inch-diameter cannoli tube, sealing the edges with a bit of egg white.
  5. Heat about 2 inches of oil to 360 degrees F in a large, heavy saucepan. Fry the shells, a few at a time, until golden, about 2 minutes. Using tongs, transfer to paper towels to drain.
  6. For the filling, combine the ricotta, confectioners' sugar, and vanilla in a bowl until smooth. Fold in the chocolate chips and orange zest.
  7. Just before serving, use a pastry bag without a tip to pipe the ricotta filling into the cannoli shells from both ends. Dust with additional confectioners' sugar and serve immediately.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 45 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour
