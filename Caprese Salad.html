Caprese Salad

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by slicing the tomatoes and mozzarella into approximately 1/4-inch slices.
  2. Next, arrange them alternately on a plate. Tuck whole basil leaves in between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Just before serving, drizzle the olive oil and balsamic vinegar mixture over the tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes

Enjoy your Caprese Salad!
