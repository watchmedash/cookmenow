Carrot Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

For the Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the oil, sugar, eggs, carrots, pineapple, and vanilla until well combined.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on a wire rack.
  8. For the frosting, in a large bowl, beat together the cream cheese, butter, confectioners' sugar, and vanilla until smooth and fluffy.
  9. Place one cake layer on a serving platter, spread frosting over it, then top with the second layer. Frost the top and sides of the cake.
  10. Refrigerate for an hour to allow the frosting to set before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes (plus cooling and chilling time)

Enjoy your Carrot Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting!
