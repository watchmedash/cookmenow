Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter. Press mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, 1 cup sugar, and vanilla. Beat until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add sour cream, flour, and milk. Beat until smooth.
  4. Pour cream cheese mixture over the crust in the pan.
  5. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until the center is set and the top appears dull.
  6. Cool in pan for 10 minutes. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Run a knife around the edge of the pan to loosen the cake before removing the sides of the pan.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 5 hours (including chilling time)
