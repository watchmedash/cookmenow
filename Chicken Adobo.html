Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 8-10 pieces chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  2. Add the garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Cook for another minute.
  3. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and water to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Remove the bay leaves and serve the chicken adobo over steamed rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour
