Chicken Binakol

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 4 cups coconut water
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, thinly sliced
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, pounded
  • 1 whole garlic, crushed
  • 2 green papaya, quartered
  • 1 cup malunggay leaves (or spinach as a substitute)
  • 1 whole coconut, meat scraped out
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the chicken, coconut water, chicken stock, onion, ginger, lemongrass, and garlic. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 30 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked.
  2. Add the papaya and coconut meat to the pot and cook for another 10-15 minutes, or until the papaya is tender.
  3. Add the malunggay leaves and season with salt and pepper. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Once the soup is done, you can optionally serve it in a hollowed-out coconut shell for an authentic Filipino presentation.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 50 minutes
  • Total Time: 70 minutes

Enjoy your Chicken Binakol!
