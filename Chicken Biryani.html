Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1kg Chicken, cut into pieces
  • 4 Onions, finely sliced
  • 3 Tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 Green chillies, slit
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tsp Biryani masala powder
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 4 cups Water
  • 3 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice under running cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water and set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and add sliced onions. Fry the onions until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies and fry for a minute.
  4. Add the chicken and cook on high heat for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and yogurt and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add biryani masala powder and salt and cook until oil starts to separate from the sides.
  7. Drain the rice and add it to the pan along with water, fresh cilantro, and mint leaves. Cover the pan and cook on low heat until the rice is cooked, for approximately 20 minutes.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 40 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 5 minutes
