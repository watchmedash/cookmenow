Chicken Caldereta

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 can liver spread
  • 2 cups potato, quartered
  • 2 cups carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup bell peppers, cut into strips
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup cheese, grated
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce (optional)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat. Saut√© garlic and onions until softened.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, liver spread, and hot sauce (if using). Stir well to coat the chicken with the sauce.
  4. Add enough water to cover the chicken and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and let it simmer until the chicken is half-cooked.
  5. Add the potatoes and carrots. Continue to simmer until the vegetables and chicken are fully cooked.
  6. Add the bell peppers, green peas, and cheese. Stir well and continue to cook for a few more minutes until the cheese is melted and the sauce has thickened.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Transfer to a serving dish and serve hot with rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 60 minutes

Enjoy your Chicken Caldereta!
