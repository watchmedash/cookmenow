Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 8 tortillas
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish
  • Sliced avocado for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large pan, saut√© onion, bell pepper, and garlic until tender. Stir in shredded chicken, half of the enchilada sauce, and 1/2 cup of cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spoon the chicken mixture evenly onto each tortilla. Roll up the tortillas and place them seam side down in a baking dish.
  4. Pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas and sprinkle with the rest of the cheese.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with sour cream, chopped cilantro, and avocado slices.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes

Enjoy your Chicken Enchiladas!
