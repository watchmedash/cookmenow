Chicken Inasal

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 stalk lemongrass
  • 1 cup coconut vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons annatto oil

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine coconut vinegar, minced garlic, brown sugar, salt, and ground black pepper. Stir until sugar and salt are dissolved.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours or overnight for best results.
  3. Prepare the grill. While the grill is heating, prepare the basting sauce by combining annatto oil and salt.
  4. Grill the chicken pieces over medium heat. Baste with the annatto oil mixture regularly to keep the meat moist.
  5. Grill the chicken until well done, with a slightly charred skin.
  6. Best served with steamed rice and a dipping sauce of soy sauce, vinegar, and chopped chili.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes (plus marinating time)
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes (plus marinating time)
