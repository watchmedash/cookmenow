Chicken Katsu Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 2 cups Japanese curry sauce
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Dredge each piece of chicken in flour, then dip in the beaten eggs, and finally coat in the panko breadcrumbs.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the breaded chicken and cook for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Remove from the pan and let drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
  3. While the chicken is cooking, heat the Japanese curry sauce according to package instructions.
  4. To serve, slice the chicken into thin strips and serve over steamed rice. Pour the curry sauce over the top.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes

Enjoy your Chicken Katsu Curry!
