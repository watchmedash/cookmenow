Chicken Marsala

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup chopped shallots
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh Italian parsley

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper, then dredge in flour. Shake off excess.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add chicken and cook until browned. Remove chicken and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add butter, shallots, and mushrooms. Cook until mushrooms are brown and tender.
  4. Add Marsala wine and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until wine is reduced by half.
  5. Add stock and simmer until it reduces by half.
  6. Return chicken to skillet. Cook, while basting with sauce, until chicken is cooked through.
  7. Plate chicken, topped with mushrooms and sauce. Garnish with chopped parsley before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes
