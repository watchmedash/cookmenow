Chicken Menudo

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs, cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup pork liver, diced (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup carrots, diced
  • 1/2 cup potatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat the cooking oil over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© the minced garlic and chopped onions until the onions become translucent.
  3. Add the diced chicken and pork liver (if using) to the pan. Cook until the chicken is lightly browned on all sides.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce, vinegar, and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cover the pan and let it cook for about 10 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
  5. Add the diced carrots, potatoes, red bell pepper, and cooked chickpeas to the pan. Stir well to combine.
  6. Continue to simmer for another 8-10 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked and tender.
  7. Pour in the tomato sauce and green peas. Stir and cook for an additional 5 minutes to let the flavors meld together.
  8. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste preference.
  9. Remove the pan from the heat and transfer the Chicken Menudo to a serving dish.
  10. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy this delicious and hearty Filipino dish!

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes

Indulge in the comforting flavors of Chicken Menudo, a Filipino classic that combines tender chicken, vegetables, and a flavorful sauce!
