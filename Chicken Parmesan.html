Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Dredge chicken in flour, then dip into the beaten eggs, and coat in breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan.
  2. Heat oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until golden, about 2 minutes on each side.
  3. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce over each chicken breast. Top with mozzarella cheese and bake until cheese is melted and chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle with fresh basil before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes
