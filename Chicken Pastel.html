Chicken Pastel

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 2 pieces chorizo de bilbao, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 large carrot, cubed
  • 2 medium potatoes, cubed
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 cup mushroom, quartered
  • 1 cup all-purpose cream
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 piece chicken bouillon cube (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat. Saut√© garlic and onions until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and chorizo de bilbao. Cook until chicken pieces are lightly browned.
  3. Add carrots, potatoes, and chicken bouillon cube. Stir and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add chicken stock and bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce heat and let it simmer until chicken is fully cooked and vegetables are tender.
  5. Add the green peas, bell peppers, and mushrooms. Cook for a few more minutes.
  6. Stir in the all-purpose cream. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for a few more minutes.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot with a side of steamed rice or bread.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 60 minutes

Enjoy your Chicken Pastel!
