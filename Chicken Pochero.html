Chicken Pochero

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 piece chorizo Bilbao, sliced (optional)
  • 2 large potatoes, quartered
  • 2 large carrots, cut into thick round slices
  • 1 small head of cabbage, quartered
  • 1 bunch of pechay or bok choy
  • 1 can (15 ounces) garbanzos (chickpeas), drained
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 to 5 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can (8 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot. Saut√© garlic and onions until softened.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add chorizo Bilbao (if using) and cook for a few more minutes.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and chicken broth. Add the whole peppercorns. Bring to a boil.
  5. Lower the heat, cover the pot, and simmer for about 20-30 minutes.
  6. Add the potatoes and carrots. Continue to cook until vegetables are tender.
  7. Add the cabbage, pechay or bok choy, and garbanzos. Cook until the greens are wilted and the cabbage is slightly tender.
  8. Season with salt to taste. Serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 1 hour 15 minutes

Enjoy your Chicken Pochero!
