Chicken Shawarma

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thigh, boneless and skinless
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups mixed salad
  • 4 pita breads

Instructions

  1. Mix the spices, garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil in a bowl. Add the chicken thighs and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium heat. Cook the chicken for 5-6 minutes on each side or until cooked through.
  3. Rest the chicken for a few minutes before slicing. Serve the sliced chicken in pita bread with salad.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 1 hour 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 12 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 22 minutes
