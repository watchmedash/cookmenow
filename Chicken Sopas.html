Chicken Sopas

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 cup elbow macaroni
  • 1 small cabbage, chopped
  • 1 can (12 ounces) evaporated milk
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add onions and garlic and sauté until onions are translucent and garlic is fragrant.
  2. Add chicken breasts to the pot and cook until no longer pink. Remove chicken and set aside to cool. Once cooled, shred the chicken into smaller pieces.
  3. In the same pot, add carrots and celery. Sauté until vegetables are slightly tender.
  4. Add the chicken broth to the pot and bring to a boil. Once boiling, add the elbow macaroni and cook until tender, about 8-10 minutes.
  5. Return the shredded chicken to the pot. Add the chopped cabbage and pour in the evaporated milk. Stir to combine all the ingredients. Let it simmer for another 5 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir once more and then remove from heat.
  7. Ladle into bowls and serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes

Enjoy your Chicken Sopas!
