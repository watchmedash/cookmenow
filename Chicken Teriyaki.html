Chicken Teriyaki

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup mirin (sweet Japanese rice wine)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch dissolved in 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the soy sauce, mirin, and sugar. Stir until the sugar is dissolved. Add the cornstarch mixture and stir until the sauce thickens. Remove from heat and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides. Reduce the heat to low and pour the teriyaki sauce over the chicken. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and slice into strips. Serve the chicken with the remaining teriyaki sauce, garnished with sesame seeds and green onions.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes

Enjoy your Chicken Teriyaki!
