Chicken Tinola

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 unripe papaya or chayote, cut into wedges
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, peeled and sliced
  • 5 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1-2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 bunch sili leaves (or spinach if not available)
  • 7 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat oil and saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until slightly brown.
  3. Add fish sauce and continue to cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add water and bring to a boil.
  5. Lower the heat and let it simmer until chicken is tender.
  6. Add the papaya or chayote and cook until tender.
  7. Add the sili leaves (or spinach) and simmer for a minute or two.
  8. Adjust seasoning if necessary. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
  • Total Time: 55 minutes
