Chicken Yakitori

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped
  • Bamboo skewers, soaked in water

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine the soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar. Bring to a boil over medium heat and stir until the sugar has dissolved. Lower the heat and simmer until the sauce has thickened, about 20 minutes.
  2. Preheat a grill or broiler. Thread the chicken pieces onto the bamboo skewers. Brush with the sauce and grill for about 5 minutes. Turn the skewers, brush with more sauce, and grill for another 5 minutes or until the chicken is cooked and slightly charred.
  3. Transfer the skewers to a platter and sprinkle with chopped green onions. Serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes

Enjoy your Chicken Yakitori!
