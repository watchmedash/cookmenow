Chicken and Dumplings

Ingredients

  • 1 large chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 quarts water
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup shortening
  • 1/2 cup ice water
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken and 2 quarts of water to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until the chicken is tender and the meat easily pulls away from the bone. Remove the chicken from the pot, let it cool, and then remove the meat from the bones. Discard the bones and skin.
  2. While the chicken is cooling, make the dumplings: In a large bowl, mix the flour and 1 teaspoon of salt. Cut in the shortening until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in the ice water a tablespoon at a time, until the dough holds together. On a floured surface, roll out the dough to 1/8 inch thickness. Cut into 2 inch squares.
  3. Return the chicken meat to the pot and bring to a simmer. Drop the dumplings into the simmering broth. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes, until the dumplings are cooked through. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 2 hours

Enjoy your Chicken and Dumplings!
