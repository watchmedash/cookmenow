Chiffon Cake

Ingredients

  • 7 large eggs, separated
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup cold water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C). Do not grease the chiffon cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, 1 cup of sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together egg yolks, water, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until smooth and well combined.
  5. In a clean, dry bowl, beat egg whites with cream of tartar until soft peaks form. Gradually add the remaining 1/2 cup sugar, continuing to beat until stiff peaks form.
  6. Gently fold the egg whites into the batter, taking care not to deflate the mixture.
  7. Pour the batter into an ungreased chiffon cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  8. Bake for 55-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
  9. Immediately invert the pan (turn it upside down) and allow the cake to cool completely. This helps keep its height.
  10. Once cooled, carefully run a knife around the edges of the pan to release the cake.
  11. Serve plain or with fresh fruit and whipped cream, if desired.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 25 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 55-60 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 20-25 minutes
