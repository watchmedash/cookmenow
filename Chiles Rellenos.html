Chiles Rellenos

Ingredients

  • 4 large poblano peppers
  • 1 cup Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • For the tomato sauce:
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Roast the poblano peppers over a gas flame or under the broiler until blackened all over. Let cool, peel off the skin, and make a small slit in each pepper. Remove the seeds and stuff with cheese.
  2. To make the tomato sauce, blend the tomatoes, onion, garlic, chicken bouillon, and water until smooth. Pour into a saucepan and simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form. In a separate bowl, beat the egg yolks. Fold the yolks into the whites. Coat each stuffed pepper with flour, then dip into the egg mixture.
  4. Heat the oil in a large frying pan. Fry the peppers until golden brown on all sides. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Serve the chiles rellenos with the tomato sauce.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour
