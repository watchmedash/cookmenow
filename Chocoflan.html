Chocoflan

Ingredients

  • For the cake:
  • 170g of all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon of salt
  • 115g of unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 200g of granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 75g of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 240ml of buttermilk
  • For the flan:
  • 1 can (400g) of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (400g) of evaporated milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • For the caramel:
  • 200g of granulated sugar
  • 60ml of water

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare a bundt pan by greasing it thoroughly.
  2. Prepare the caramel by mixing sugar and water in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir until sugar is fully dissolved. Continue cooking without stirring until the mixture turns a golden color. Pour the caramel into the bundt pan and set aside.
  3. For the cake, whisk together flour, baking powder, salt, and cocoa in a bowl. In a separate bowl, beat the butter and sugar until creamy. Add the egg and vanilla, beating until well incorporated. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, alternating with the buttermilk.
  4. Pour the cake batter over the caramel in the bundt pan, spreading evenly.
  5. For the flan, blend the condensed milk, evaporated milk, eggs, and vanilla in a blender until smooth. Pour this mixture over the cake batter.
  6. Cover the bundt pan with foil and place it in a larger roasting pan. Pour hot water into the roasting pan until it comes halfway up the sides of the bundt pan, creating a water bath.
  7. Bake for about 1 hour or until a toothpick inserted into the cake comes out clean. Let it cool completely in the pan, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight.
  8. When ready to serve, gently loosen the edges of the cake with a knife and invert onto a large plate.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour
  • Cooling Time: 4 hours
  • Total Time: 5 hours 30 minutes

Enjoy your Chocoflan!
