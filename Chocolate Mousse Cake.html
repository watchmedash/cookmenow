Chocolate Mousse Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

For the Chocolate Mousse:

  • 8 oz semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla until smooth. Gradually add the dry ingredients until just combined.
  4. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans and smooth the tops with a spatula.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on a wire rack.
  7. For the mousse, melt the chocolate in a heatproof bowl over simmering water or in the microwave. Let cool slightly.
  8. Whip the cream, sugar, and vanilla until stiff peaks form. Gently fold in the melted chocolate until well combined.
  9. Place one cake layer on a serving platter, spread half the mousse over it, then top with the second layer. Spread the remaining mousse over the top and sides of the cake.
  10. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours to allow the mousse to set before slicing and serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 35 minutes
  • Total Time: 65 minutes (plus cooling and chilling time)

Enjoy your Chocolate Mousse Cake!
