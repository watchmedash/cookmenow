Classic Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the lettuce, then tear it into bite-sized pieces and place in a large salad bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and minced garlic. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Pour the dressing over the lettuce and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Add the Parmesan cheese and croutons to the salad and toss again.
  5. Serve the salad immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan if desired.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 0 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes
