Coconut Cream Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 box white cake mix
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup sweetened shredded coconut
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the cake mix, eggs, water, and oil on medium speed for 2 minutes. Stir in 1/2 cup of coconut milk.
  3. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans and smooth the tops with a spatula.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. While the cake is still warm, poke holes in the top of the cake layers with a fork, then pour the remaining 1/2 cup of coconut milk over the cakes. Allow to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on a wire rack.
  6. In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream with the powdered sugar and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  7. Place one cake layer on a serving platter. Spread a layer of whipped cream over the cake, then sprinkle with shredded coconut. Top with the second cake layer, then frost the top and sides of the cake with the remaining whipped cream. Garnish with additional shredded coconut.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes (plus chilling time)

Enjoy your Coconut Cream Cake!
