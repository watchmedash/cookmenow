Coq au Riesling

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 chicken, cut into 8 pieces
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 medium onions, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 8 oz (225 g) pancetta, diced
  • 8 oz (225 g) button mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups dry Riesling wine
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large heavy-bottomed pan over medium-high heat. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper and brown them in the pan on all sides. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add the onions, garlic, and pancetta. Cook until the onions are translucent and the pancetta is crispy. Add the mushrooms and continue to cook until they are softened.
  3. Add the Riesling wine to the pan, scraping the bottom to deglaze. Return the chicken to the pan and bring the mixture to a boil. Lower the heat, cover the pan, and let it simmer for about 20 minutes.
  4. Uncover the pan and stir in the cream. Let it simmer for an additional 10 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce is thickened. Season to taste with more salt and pepper if needed.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour

Enjoy your Coq au Riesling!
