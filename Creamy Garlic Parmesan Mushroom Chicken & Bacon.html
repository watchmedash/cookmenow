Creamy Garlic Parmesan Mushroom Chicken & Bacon

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts (pounded 1/2-inch thin)
  • 2 tablespoons each of olive oil and butter
  • 6 cloves garlic, finely diced
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 1/3 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • 1/2 teaspoon each of salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. In a pan over medium-high heat, melt butter with the oil. Add the chicken and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through. Remove chicken and set aside on a plate.
  2. In the same pan, add the garlic and onion and cook for a minute until fragrant. Add the mushrooms, salt and pepper then cook until mushrooms begin to soften.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and allow to simmer for a couple of minutes, or until the broth has reduced by half.
  4. Reduce heat to low-medium heat, add the cream and bring to a gentle simmer, while stirring occasionally. Add in the Parmesan cheese, allow sauce to simmer for another minute until cheese melts through the sauce.
  5. Add the cooked chicken back into the pan; sprinkle with extra herbs if desired. Serve immediately.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
