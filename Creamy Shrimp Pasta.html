Creamy Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound large shrimp
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese grated
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons parsley chopped

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil over medium high heat in a large pan.
  3. Add the shrimp to the pan and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side or until pink and opaque.
  4. Remove the shrimp from the pan and set aside on a plate.
  5. Add the garlic to the pan and cook for 30 seconds.
  6. Add the cream, Italian seasoning and parmesan cheese to the pan. Simmer for 3-4 minutes or until sauce has started to thicken.
  7. Drain the pasta and place in the pan with the cream sauce. Toss to coat the pasta with the sauce.
  8. Add the shrimp back to the pan and simmer for 2-3 minutes or until heated through. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
