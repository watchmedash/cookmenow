Crispy Baked Zucchini Fries

Ingredients

  • 2 large zucchinis
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Olive oil spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Trim the ends of the zucchinis and cut them into fry-like strips, about 1/2 inch thick and 3-4 inches long.
  3. In three separate shallow bowls, place the all-purpose flour, beaten eggs, and a mixture of panko breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Dip each zucchini strip into the flour, then into the beaten eggs, and finally coat them thoroughly with the breadcrumb mixture.
  5. Place the coated zucchini fries on the prepared baking sheet in a single layer, leaving space between each fry.
  6. Lightly spray the zucchini fries with olive oil to help them crisp up in the oven.
  7. Bake the zucchini fries for 18-20 minutes or until they are golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove the baked zucchini fries from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.
  9. Serve the crispy baked zucchini fries with your favorite dipping sauce, such as marinara or ranch dressing.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 18-20 minutes
  • Total Time: 33-35 minutes

Enjoy your delicious and healthier alternative to traditional fries!
