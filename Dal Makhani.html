Dal Makhani

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole black lentils (urad dal)
  • 1/4 cup red kidney beans (rajma)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt, to taste
  • Chopped cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak the lentils and beans overnight. Rinse and pressure cook with 4 cups of water and salt for 15 minutes.
  2. In a pan, heat the butter and add cumin seeds. Once they start to splutter, add onions and green chilies and sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears. Then add the tomato puree, turmeric, coriander, and chili powder. Cook until oil starts separating from the mixture.
  4. Add the cooked lentils and beans to the mixture and let it simmer for 15 minutes. If the mixture is too thick, add some water.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and let it cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 1 hour 20 minutes

Enjoy your Dal Makhani!
