Doro Wat

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chicken drumsticks and thighs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • ¼ cup clarified butter (niter kibbeh or ghee)
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh ginger
  • ¼ cup Ethiopian Berbere spice mix
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 cup red wine
  • ½ cup water
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken pieces with salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the clarified butter over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until they are very soft and starting to brown, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic, ginger, Berbere spice mix, and paprika. Stir well and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and turn them in the spice mixture until they are well coated.
  5. Add the wine and water to the pan. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover the pan, and simmer for about 45 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors are well blended.
  6. Add the hard-boiled eggs and cook for another 10 minutes. The eggs should be well coated with the sauce.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Enjoy your Doro Wat!
