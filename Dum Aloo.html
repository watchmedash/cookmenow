Dum Aloo

Ingredients

  • 500g baby potatoes
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chillies, slit
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 2 tsp cumin powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 cup yogurt, whisked
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the baby potatoes until they are cooked. Peel and prick them all over with a fork. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add the boiled potatoes and fry until golden. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same oil, add chopped onions, ginger-garlic paste and green chillies. Saut√© until onions turn golden brown.
  4. Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, cumin powder and coriander powder. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Lower the heat and add whisked yogurt, stirring continuously until it comes to a boil.
  6. Add the fried potatoes, garam masala and salt. Cover and cook on low heat for about 10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 55 minutes
