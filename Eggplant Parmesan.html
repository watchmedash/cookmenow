Eggplant Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Slice the eggplants into 1/2 inch thick rounds. Sprinkle with salt and let sit for 15 minutes.
  2. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  3. Dip each eggplant slice in flour, then in the beaten eggs, and finally in the panko breadcrumbs mixed with oregano and pepper.
  4. Fry each slice in oil until golden brown and crispy, then drain on paper towels.
  5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Layer the eggplant slices in the dish, top with more sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and browned on top. Sprinkle with fresh basil before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour

Enjoy your Eggplant Parmesan!
