Eggs Benedict

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 English muffins, halved
  • 4 slices of ham or bacon
  • 2 tablespoons of white vinegar
  • For Hollandaise sauce:
    • 2 egg yolks
    • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, melted
    • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
    • Pinch of salt
    • Pinch of cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. To prepare Hollandaise sauce, whisk together egg yolks, lemon juice, salt, and cayenne pepper in a heatproof bowl. Set the bowl over a saucepan of barely simmering water and continue to whisk until the mixture is thick enough to coat the back of a spoon. Gradually add melted butter, whisking continuously. Remove from heat and keep warm.
  2. Bring a large saucepan of water to a boil. Add vinegar and reduce the heat to low. Carefully crack eggs into the water and cook for 3-4 minutes for soft poached eggs. Using a slotted spoon, remove eggs from the water and set aside.
  3. Toast the English muffins and place a slice of ham or bacon on each half. Top with a poached egg and spoon over the Hollandaise sauce.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
