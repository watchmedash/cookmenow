Enchiladas Verdes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups green enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 12 small corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup chopped white onion

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until no longer pink inside, about 5-7 minutes per side. Remove from heat and let cool, then shred the chicken with two forks.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the enchilada sauce and sour cream. Dip a tortilla into the sauce, then place on a flat surface. In the middle of the tortilla, add some of the shredded chicken, a sprinkle of cheese, and a bit of chopped onion. Roll up the tortilla and place seam side down in a baking dish. Repeat with the remaining tortillas.
  3. Pour the remaining sauce over the rolled enchiladas in the baking dish. Sprinkle with the remaining cheese.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and starting to brown.
  5. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped cilantro.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 35 minutes
  • Total Time: 55 minutes

Enjoy your Enchiladas Verdes!
