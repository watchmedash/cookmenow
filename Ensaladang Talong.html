Ensaladang Talong (Eggplant Salad)

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggplants
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cane vinegar or white vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Grill the eggplants until the skin is charred and blistered. You can also broil them in the oven.
  2. Let them cool, then peel off the skin under running water.
  3. Chop the eggplants and place them in a bowl.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and onion.
  5. Drizzle with vinegar and season with salt and pepper. Mix well.
  6. Refrigerate for at least an hour before serving to allow the flavors to blend together.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
