Feijoada

Ingredients

  • 500g black beans
  • 200g pork shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 2 sausages, sliced
  • 200g bacon, cut into chunks
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 5 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • White rice, for serving
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak black beans in a large bowl of water overnight, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, add beans, pork shoulder, sausages, bacon, onions, garlic, and bay leaves. Add water until everything is well covered.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 2 hours, or until the beans are cooked and the meats are very tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with white rice, garnished with fresh parsley.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 2 hours
  • Total Time: 2 hours 20 minutes
