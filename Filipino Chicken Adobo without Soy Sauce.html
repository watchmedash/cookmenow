Filipino Chicken Adobo without Soy Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorn
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Combine chicken, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, peppercorn and salt in a bowl. Marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook until brown on all sides.
  3. Add the marinade and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  4. Adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 1 hour 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 55 minutes
