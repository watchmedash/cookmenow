Filipino Chicken Barbecue

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup of calamansi juice or lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup of 7UP, Sprite, or beer (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of brown or white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of banana or tomato ketchup

Instructions

  1. In a large container, combine soy sauce, minced garlic, chopped onions, calamansi juice, ground black pepper, and sugar. Stir well until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add the chicken thighs to the container. Make sure all the chicken pieces are covered with the marinade. Marinate the chicken for at least 3 hours or overnight for best results.
  3. If using 7UP, Sprite, or beer, add it to the marinated chicken around 30 minutes before grilling.
  4. Prepare the grill for direct grilling. Over medium heat, grill the chicken thighs for 10 to 12 minutes on each side or until fully cooked. Baste the chicken thighs with ketchup while grilling.
  5. Remove the chicken thighs from the grill and serve with rice and pickled papaya or your favorite side dish.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 3 hours (including marination)
  • Cooking Time: 20-24 minutes
  • Total Time: Approximately 3.5 hours

Enjoy your Filipino Chicken Barbecue!
