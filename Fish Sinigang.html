Chicken Afritada

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, quartered
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 medium-sized carrot, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup green peas (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© the garlic and onions until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken to the pan and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, soy sauce, and water. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Lower the heat, cover the pan, and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Add the potatoes and carrots. Cook for another 15 minutes or until the potatoes and carrots are tender.
  6. Add the bell peppers and green peas. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well.
  8. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour
