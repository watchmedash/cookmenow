Fish Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 lb white fish fillets
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornmeal
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425F (220C). In a shallow dish, combine flour, cornmeal, paprika, salt and pepper.
  2. Dip fish fillets in buttermilk, then coat with flour mixture. Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
  4. While the fish is baking, warm the tortillas in the oven or on the stove. Prepare the vegetables.
  5. Assemble the tacos by placing the baked fish on a tortilla, then topping with lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and cilantro.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
