Fresh Lumpia (Fresh Spring Rolls)

Ingredients

  • For the Spring Rolls:
    • 2 cups sweet potato, julienned or grated
    • 2 cups baby shrimp
    • 1 large carrot, julienned
    • 1 cup green beans, julienned and blanched
    • 1 cup cabbage, thinly sliced
    • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 1 onion, thinly sliced
    • 1 lettuce leaf (per roll)
    • 8-10 pieces of Lumpia Wrapper
    • 2 tablespoons of oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Sauce:
    • 1 cup brown sugar
    • 2 cups vegetable broth
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 2 tablespoons cornstarch (diluted in 4 tablespoons of water)
    • 5 cloves of garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. In a pan over medium heat, add oil. Sauté the garlic and onions until translucent.
  2. Add the shrimp and cook until color changes to orange.
  3. Add sweet potato, carrots, and green beans. Sauté for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the cabbage and season with salt and pepper. Sauté until all vegetables are cooked but still crunchy. Set aside and let it cool.
  5. Prepare your lumpia wrapper on a flat surface. Place a lettuce leaf at the center, then add the vegetable filling.
  6. Fold and roll your lumpia. Make sure it’s tight and secure.
  7. For the sauce: Combine brown sugar, soy sauce, and vegetable broth in a saucepan. Bring it to a boil.
  8. Dilute the cornstarch in water and pour into the saucepan. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens.
  9. Place minced garlic on top of the sauce.
  10. Drizzle the sauce on top of the Lumpiang Sariwa before serving. Enjoy!

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour
