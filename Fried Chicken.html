Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken pieces in a large bowl and pour the buttermilk over them. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least one hour, or up to 24 hours.
  2. When ready to cook, mix together the flour, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl. Take each piece of chicken out of the buttermilk, allowing excess to drip off, then roll in the flour mixture until well coated.
  3. Fill a large, deep skillet with enough oil to fully submerge the chicken pieces. Heat the oil to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Fry the chicken pieces, a few at a time so as not to crowd the pan, for about 10 minutes, until golden brown and cooked through. Drain on paper towels.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 1 hour 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 55 minutes

Enjoy your Fried Chicken!
