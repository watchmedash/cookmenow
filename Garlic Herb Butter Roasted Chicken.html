Garlic Herb Butter Roasted Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4 pounds)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 4-6 garlic cloves, whole
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425째F (220째C). Line a roasting pan with foil for easy cleanup.
  2. In a bowl, mix together softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, lemon zest, salt, and black pepper to make the garlic herb butter.
  3. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels, inside and out. Gently loosen the skin of the chicken with your fingers, being careful not to tear it.
  4. Spread about two-thirds of the garlic herb butter mixture under the chicken skin, covering as much of the breast and thighs as possible.
  5. Rub the remaining butter mixture all over the outside of the chicken.
  6. Place the lemon slices, quartered onion, and whole garlic cloves inside the cavity of the chicken.
  7. Tie the chicken legs together with kitchen twine to help it cook evenly.
  8. Drizzle the chicken with a little olive oil, and sprinkle with additional salt and black pepper.
  9. Place the chicken in the roasting pan breast-side up.
  10. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for about 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C) when measured with a meat thermometer.
  11. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before carving.
  12. Serve the Garlic Herb Butter Roasted Chicken with your favorite sides and enjoy a flavorful and juicy roast chicken dinner!

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Indulge in the succulent and aromatic Garlic Herb Butter Roasted Chicken for a comforting and satisfying meal!
