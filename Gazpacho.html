Gazpacho (Spanish Cold Tomato Soup)

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 purple onion, minced
  • 1 cucumber, peeled, seeded, chopped
  • 1 sweet red bell pepper (or green) seeded and chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1-2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Salt and fresh ground pepper to taste
  • 6 or more drops of hot sauce, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • 1 can (48 oz) tomato juice
  • Crusty bread, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients except the tomato juice in a large bowl.
  2. Using a blender or food processor, purée the mixture until it's smooth and well blended. Leave it a bit chunky if you prefer it that way.
  3. Transfer the puréed mixture to another bowl and add the tomato juice until it reaches your desired consistency.
  4. Chill the gazpacho in the fridge for at least 2 hours before serving. It can be chilled overnight if preferred.
  5. Serve with a drizzle of olive oil on top and some crusty bread on the side if desired.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Chilling Time: 2 hours
  • Total Time: 2 hours 20 minutes

Enjoy your Gazpacho!
