German Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds small red potatoes, cut into quarters
  • 6 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Place the potatoes in a large pot of salted water and bring to a boil. Cook until tender, about 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until crisp. Remove the bacon and set aside, leaving the drippings in the skillet.
  3. Add the onion to the skillet and cook until softened.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the vinegar, water, sugar, salt, and pepper. Add this mixture to the skillet and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat and simmer for 2-3 minutes until slightly thickened.
  6. Add the cooked potatoes to the skillet, tossing to coat with the dressing. Cook for a few more minutes until heated through.
  7. Stir in the cooked bacon and parsley, then transfer to a serving dish.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes

Enjoy your German Potato Salad!
