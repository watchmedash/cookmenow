Ginataang Manok

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 ginger root, sliced
  • 2 chili peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat, saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the chili peppers, coconut milk, and water. Stir well and bring to a boil.
  4. Lower the heat and let it simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened, about 20 minutes.
  5. Add the spinach leaves and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes

Enjoy your Ginataang Manok!
