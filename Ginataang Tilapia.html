Ginataang Tilapia

Ingredients

  • 2 tilapia, cleaned and scales removed
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 2 long green chilli peppers
  • 2 medium-sized eggplant, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger until they become aromatic.
  2. Add the cleaned tilapia to the pan. Cook each side for about 2 minutes.
  3. Pour the coconut milk into the pan. Bring it to a boil.
  4. Once boiling, lower the heat to a simmer. Add the sliced eggplants and long green chilli peppers. Cover the pan and let it simmer for about 15 minutes. This allows the flavors to infuse with the coconut milk.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Make sure to gently stir the mixture to prevent the coconut milk from curdling.
  6. Continue to simmer until the coconut milk has reduced and thickened slightly.
  7. Turn off the heat. Transfer the dish to a serving plate.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes

Enjoy your Ginataang Tilapia!
