Goulash

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
  • 1.5 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1.5 cups beef broth
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, and cook until translucent.
  2. Add the paprika and caraway seeds, stirring well to combine. Add the beef, stirring to coat the beef with the spice mixture.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and beef broth, bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for about 1 hour.
  4. Add the potatoes and bell peppers, cover and simmer for an additional 30 minutes, or until the beef is tender and the potatoes are cooked through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve the goulash hot with a side of crusty bread.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour 45 minutes
  • Total Time: 2 hours

Enjoy your Goulash!
