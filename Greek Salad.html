Greek Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 200g feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup olives (green or black)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Chop the cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and bell pepper into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Place in a large salad bowl.
  3. Add the feta cheese and olives to the salad.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, then sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss the salad until everything is well combined.
  6. Chill in the fridge for 1 hour before serving to let the flavors meld together.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Chilling Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 1 hour 15 minutes
