Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 lemons, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, dried rosemary, and dried oregano to create the marinade.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them, ensuring each fillet is coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to marinate.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the marinated salmon from the dish and discard any excess marinade.
  5. Grill the salmon fillets for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the salmon is cooked through and easily flakes with a fork.
  6. While grilling, add the sliced lemons to the grill and cook for about 1-2 minutes per side until they are lightly charred.
  7. Serve the grilled salmon with the charred lemon slices on top.
  8. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Marinating Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes

Enjoy your delicious Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon!
