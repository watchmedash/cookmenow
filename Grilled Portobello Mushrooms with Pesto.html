Grilled Portobello Mushrooms with Pesto

Ingredients

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms, stems removed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup pesto sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the portobello mushrooms with olive oil on both sides and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the mushrooms for about 5-7 minutes on each side until tender and lightly charred.
  4. Remove the mushrooms from the grill and spread a generous amount of pesto sauce on the gill side of each mushroom.
  5. Top with cherry tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil.
  6. Return the mushrooms to the grill, close the lid, and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until the cheese has melted.
  7. Remove from the grill and serve immediately, garnished with additional basil if desired.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
