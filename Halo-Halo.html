Halo-Halo

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shaved ice
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened bananas
  • 2 tablespoons macapuno (sweetened coconut strips)
  • 2 tablespoons kaong (sweetened palm fruit)
  • 2 tablespoons nata de coco (sweetened coconut gel)
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened jackfruit
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened kidney beans
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened chickpeas
  • 2 tablespoons leche flan
  • 2 tablespoons ube halaya (purple yam jam)
  • 1 scoop ube ice cream
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream

Instructions

  1. Start with a tall glass. Layer the sweetened ingredients at the bottom. The order doesn't matter, but traditionally, sweetened beans and fruits are first.
  2. Add the shaved ice on top of the layered ingredients until the glass is almost full.
  3. Drizzle the sweetened condensed milk over the shaved ice.
  4. Add a scoop each of ube and vanilla ice cream on top.
  5. Finally, add the leche flan and ube halaya on top of the ice cream.
  6. Serve with a long spoon. Mix well before eating to experience the variety of flavors and textures.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes
