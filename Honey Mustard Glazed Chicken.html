Honey Mustard Glazed Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together Dijon mustard, honey, olive oil, minced garlic, soy sauce, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper to create the honey mustard glaze.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish.
  3. Pour half of the honey mustard glaze over the chicken, making sure it is evenly coated. Reserve the other half of the glaze for later.
  4. Marinate the chicken in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours, for maximum flavor.
  5. Preheat the grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.
  6. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the used marinade.
  7. Grill the chicken for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C) and is no longer pink in the center.
  8. During the last few minutes of grilling, brush the reserved honey mustard glaze on both sides of the chicken to create a flavorful glaze.
  9. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  10. Serve the honey mustard glazed chicken with your favorite side dishes and enjoy!

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Marinating Time: 30 minutes to 4 hours
  • Cooking Time: 12-14 minutes
  • Total Time: 52-64 minutes
