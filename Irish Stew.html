Irish Stew

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1.5 pounds lamb shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into large chunks
  • 3 large carrots, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 4 cups beef or chicken broth
  • 1 cup of Guinness stout (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the lamb and brown on all sides. Remove the meat and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the onions and garlic and saut√© until they start to soften.
  3. Return the meat to the pot and add the potatoes, carrots, broth, Guinness (if using), and thyme. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 2 hours, until the meat is tender and the stew has thickened.
  5. Adjust the seasoning if necessary, then serve the stew hot, garnished with parsley.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 2 hours 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 2 hours 35 minutes

Enjoy your Irish Stew!
