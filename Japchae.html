Japchae

Ingredients

  • 250g Korean sweet potato vermicelli noodles (dangmyeon)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 green onions, sliced into 1-inch lengths
  • 100g spinach, blanched and squeezed dry
  • 200g thinly sliced beef (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the sweet potato vermicelli according to the package instructions, then drain and cut into shorter lengths if desired. Toss with a splash of sesame oil to prevent sticking and set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, and minced garlic to make the sauce. Set aside.
  3. In a large frying pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. If using beef, add it to the pan and stir-fry until cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add the carrots, bell pepper, and onions. Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add the cooked noodles, sauce, beef (if using), green onions, and spinach. Stir well to combine all the ingredients and heat through.
  6. Serve the Japchae hot, garnished with a sprinkle of sesame seeds on top.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes

Enjoy your Japchae!
