Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup peas
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the onions, bell peppers, and garlic. Cook until softened.
  2. Add the thyme, curry powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, and rice. Stir well to coat the rice in the spices.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and chicken broth. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. After 20 minutes, add the peas and stir well. Cover the pot again and cook for another 10 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through and the liquid is absorbed.
  6. Season with salt to taste, and serve warm.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 35 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes
