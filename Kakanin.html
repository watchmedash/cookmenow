Kakanin

Ingredients

  • 2 cups glutinous rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup fresh grated coconut
  • banana leaves for lining the pan

Instructions

  1. Rinse and drain the glutinous rice.
  2. In a pot, combine the rice and water. Bring to a boil.
  3. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer until the water is fully absorbed by the rice.
  4. In another pot, combine coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Heat it over medium heat until it boils.
  5. Once the coconut milk mixture is boiling, add the cooked rice. Mix well to ensure that the rice is covered with the coconut milk mixture.
  6. Continue to cook over low heat, stirring regularly, until the mixture becomes very thick.
  7. Preheat your steamer. Line a baking dish with banana leaves.
  8. Transfer the cooked rice mixture into the lined baking dish, spreading it evenly.
  9. Place the baking dish in the steamer and steam for about 30 minutes, or until the kakanin is fully set.
  10. Remove from the steamer and let it cool before cutting into serving pieces. Top with fresh grated coconut before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 1 hour 15 minutes

Enjoy your Kakanin!
