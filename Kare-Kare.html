Kare-Kare

Ingredients

  • 1 kg oxtail, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 cups unsalted roasted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup glutinous rice
  • 1 large eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch bok choy
  • 1 cup long beans, cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 4 tablespoons annatto seeds
  • Salt, to taste
  • Shrimp paste (bagoong), to serve

Instructions

  1. Boil oxtail in a large pot with enough water to cover, along with onion and garlic. Simmer until tender.
  2. In a pan, toast the glutinous rice until golden brown. Grind together with peanuts in a food processor until it forms a paste.
  3. Steep annatto seeds in warm water. Strain to get annatto water and discard seeds.
  4. Add peanut-rice paste and annatto water to the pot with oxtail. Stir well until combined and bring to a boil.
  5. Add eggplant, bok choy, and long beans. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt. Serve with bagoong on the side.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 2 hours
  • Total Time: 2 hours 30 minutes
