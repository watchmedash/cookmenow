Kimchi Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 1 cup of chopped kimchi
  • 1/4 cup of kimchi juice
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons of sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 2 fried eggs
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add garlic and onion, cook until they begin to soften.
  2. Add kimchi and kimchi juice, cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add the rice, breaking up any clumps, then stir in soy sauce, gochujang, and sesame oil.
  4. Stir fry for a few minutes until everything is well combined and heated through.
  5. Divide the rice into two servings, top each with a fried egg, sprinkle with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes
