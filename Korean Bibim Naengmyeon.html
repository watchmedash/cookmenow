Korean Bibim Naengmyeon

Ingredients

  • 2 servings of naengmyeon noodles
  • 1/2 cup julienned cucumber
  • 1/2 cup julienned Korean pear
  • 2 boiled eggs, cut into halves
  • 2 tablespoons roasted sesame seeds
  • For the sauce:
  • 4 tablespoons gochujang (Korean red pepper paste)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook naengmyeon noodles according to package instructions. Once cooked, rinse the noodles under cold water until chilled. Drain well.
  2. In a bowl, combine all the sauce ingredients and mix until well combined.
  3. Place the cold noodles in serving bowls. Top with the julienned cucumber, Korean pear, and the boiled egg halves. Drizzle the sauce over the top, and sprinkle with the roasted sesame seeds.
  4. Just before eating, mix all the ingredients together. The noodles should be coated well with the sauce. Enjoy this refreshing, spicy, and tangy dish.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes

Enjoy your Korean Bibim Naengmyeon!
