Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil, divided
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch, dissolved in 2 tablespoons water
  • 3 dried chili peppers
  • 1 teaspoon Szechuan peppercorns
  • 1/2 cup unsalted dry roasted peanuts
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together the chicken, soy sauce, and cornstarch slurry. Let marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of sesame oil in a wok or large frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the chili peppers and Szechuan peppercorns and cook until fragrant. Add the chicken and stir-fry until no longer pink, about 3-5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the peanuts, green onions, garlic, rice vinegar, and hoisin sauce. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, until everything is well combined and heated through.
  4. Drizzle with the remaining tablespoon of sesame oil before serving. Serve with steamed rice, if desired.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes

Enjoy your Kung Pao Chicken!
