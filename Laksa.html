Laksa

Ingredients

  • 200g dried rice vermicelli
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 200g chicken breast, cut into thin slices
  • 200g prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 2 litres chicken stock
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved
  • 100g bean sprouts
  • 3 tablespoons laksa paste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Lime wedges, to serve

Instructions

  1. Soak the vermicelli in warm water for about 10 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the laksa paste and cook, stirring, for 1 minute or until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken stock and coconut milk to the pot. Bring to a boil.
  4. Add the chicken slices and prawns. Reduce the heat to a simmer, and cook for about 5-6 minutes, until the chicken and prawns are cooked through.
  5. Divide the vermicelli between 4 bowls. Top with the chicken, prawns, boiled eggs and bean sprouts. Ladle the hot soup over the top.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander, and serve with lime wedges on the side.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes
