Lamb Tagine

Ingredients

  • 1 kg lamb, cut into chunks
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 800g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 500ml chicken stock
  • 2 carrots, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 2 courgettes, cut into chunks
  • 100g dried apricots
  • Handful of fresh coriander, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the lamb with the ground spices and a pinch of salt. Mix well to coat the lamb in the spices.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan or tagine over medium heat. Add the onions, garlic, and ginger and cook until softened.
  3. Add the spiced lamb to the pan and brown on all sides.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and chicken stock, bring to a simmer. Cover and cook for 1 hour.
  5. Add the carrots, courgettes, and dried apricots to the pan. Cover and cook for another hour, or until the lamb is tender.
  6. Top with fresh coriander before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 2 hours
  • Total Time: 2 hours 20 minutes
