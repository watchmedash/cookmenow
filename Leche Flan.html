Leche Flan

Ingredients

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 cup of evaporated milk
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Water, for steaming

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, melt the sugar until it turns into a caramel. Carefully pour the caramel into your mold, making sure to cover the bottom evenly.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract. Stir until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the caramel-coated mold.
  4. Place the mold into a larger baking pan and fill the pan with enough water to reach halfway up the sides of the mold.
  5. Cover the mold with aluminum foil and steam over medium heat for about 40 to 60 minutes, or until the flan is set and a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  6. Let the flan cool before refrigerating for at least 2 hours. When ready to serve, turn the mold upside down onto a serving plate. Enjoy!

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 60 minutes
  • Total Time: 80 minutes
