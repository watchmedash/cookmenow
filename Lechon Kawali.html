Lechon Kawali

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into 2-inch chunks
  • 1 head garlic, crushed
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Oil, for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Combine pork belly, garlic, onion, bay leaves, peppercorns, salt, and enough water to cover in a large pot. Bring to a boil then lower the heat and let it simmer until the pork belly is tender.
  2. Once the pork belly is tender, remove it from the pot and let it dry. Refrigerate for at least an hour or overnight to ensure it's completely dry.
  3. Heat oil in a deep, heavy-bottomed pot. Deep fry the pork belly chunks until golden and crispy.
  4. Drain the fried pork belly on paper towels to remove excess oil. Serve hot with your preferred dipping sauce.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour
  • Additional Time (for drying): 1 hour to overnight
  • Total Time: Minimum of 2 hours 15 minutes
