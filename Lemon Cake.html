Lemon Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk

For the Lemon Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the butter, sugar, eggs, lemon zest, and vanilla until light and fluffy. Gradually add the dry ingredients, alternating with the milk, until just combined.
  4. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans and smooth the tops with a spatula.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on a wire rack.
  7. For the glaze, whisk together the powdered sugar, lemon juice, and zest (if using) until smooth.
  8. Place one cake layer on a serving platter, spread half the glaze over it, then top with the second layer. Spread the remaining glaze over the top of the cake.
  9. Allow the glaze to set for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes (plus cooling and glazing time)

Enjoy your Lemon Cake!
