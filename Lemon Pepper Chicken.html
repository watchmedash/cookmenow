Lemon Pepper Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, lemon zest, black pepper, and salt. Stir well to combine.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish and pour the marinade over the chicken. Turn the chicken to ensure it is fully coated in the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or up to overnight.
  3. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and no longer pink in the center.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Marinating Time: 1 hour
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 40 minutes
