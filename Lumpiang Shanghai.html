Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients

  • 500g ground chicken
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 30 lumpia wrappers
  • Oil for frying
  • Sweet and sour sauce for dipping

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine ground chicken, carrot, onion, garlic, egg, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Place about a tablespoon of the mixture on one end of a lumpia wrapper, roll tightly, and seal the edges with water.
  3. Repeat with the remaining mixture and lumpia wrappers.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the lumpia in batches until golden brown, about 3-5 minutes each side.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve with sweet and sour sauce.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes

Enjoy your Lumpiang Shanghai!
