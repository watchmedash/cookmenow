Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch paprika

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni according to the package directions. Drain.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat. Stir in enough flour to absorb all of the butter. Slowly pour milk into butter-flour mixture while continuously stirring until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes. Add Cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese to the milk-butter mixture and stir until it is melted and smooth.
  3. Stir in cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce to coat.
  4. Pour the mac and cheese into a baking dish. Stir the breadcrumbs and 2 tablespoons butter together and spread over the mac and cheese. Sprinkle with paprika.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 30 minutes, or until it's hot and bubbling and the top is golden and crispy.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes
