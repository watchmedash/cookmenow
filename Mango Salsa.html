Mango Salsa

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes, diced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeds removed and finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 tablespoon honey or agave nectar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Tortilla chips, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced mangoes, red bell pepper, red onion, jalapeño, and cilantro.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, honey or agave nectar, and salt until well combined.
  3. Pour the lime dressing over the mango mixture and gently toss to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  4. Refrigerate the mango salsa for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve the mango salsa with tortilla chips for a refreshing and delicious appetizer or snack.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes (including chilling time)

Enjoy your fresh and tangy Mango Salsa!
