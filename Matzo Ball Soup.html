Matzo Ball Soup

Ingredients

  • For the matzo balls:
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup chicken fat (or vegetable oil)
  • 1/4 cup seltzer water or chicken broth
  • 1 cup matzo meal
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • For the soup:
  • 2 liters chicken broth
  • 3 carrots, sliced into thin rounds
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped parsley and dill for serving

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, beat the eggs with the chicken fat (or oil) and seltzer (or chicken broth). Stir in the matzo meal, salt, and pepper. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. While the matzo ball mixture is resting, prepare the soup. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a boil. Add the carrots, celery, onion, and garlic. Reduce the heat and simmer, covered, for 30 minutes.
  3. Bring a separate pot of salted water to a boil. Form the matzo ball mixture into 1-inch balls and drop into the boiling water. Cover and simmer until the matzo balls are cooked through and fluffy, about 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  4. Add the cooked matzo balls to the soup. Season with salt and pepper. Ladle into bowls, garnish with fresh chopped parsley and dill, and serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 40 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 1 hour 40 minutes

Enjoy your Matzo Ball Soup!
