Miso Soup

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • 1 piece kombu (dried kelp), about 6 inches
  • 1/2 cup dried bonito flakes
  • 1/3 cup miso paste
  • 1/2 cup cubed tofu
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Place water and kombu in a pot and bring to a boil. Remove the kombu just before it reaches a boil.
  2. Add bonito flakes and let the water come back to a boil. Once boiling, reduce heat to low and let the flakes simmer for about 5 minutes.
  3. Strain the bonito flakes from the broth, returning the broth to the pot. Heat the broth over medium heat until it's nearly boiling, then reduce the heat to low.
  4. Place the miso paste into a small bowl. Ladle about 1/2 cup of the heated broth into the bowl and whisk until the miso paste is completely dissolved. Pour this mixture back into the pot and stir until combined.
  5. Add tofu to the pot and cook for another few minutes until heated through.
  6. Divide the soup among bowls, top with sliced green onions, and serve.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 5 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 20 minutes

Enjoy your Miso Soup!
