Moroccan Couscous

Ingredients

  • 1 cup couscous
  • 1 1/2 cups water or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. Bring the water or broth to a boil in a saucepan, stir in the couscous, cover, and remove from heat. Let stand for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  3. Add the carrot, zucchini, and bell pepper to the skillet and cook until tender.
  4. Stir in the chickpeas, turmeric, cumin, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few minutes to blend the flavors.
  5. Stir in the couscous, raisins, and cilantro, mixing well. Cook for a few more minutes, until heated through.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
