Moules-Frites (Mussels and Fries)

Ingredients

  • For the mussels:
    • 2 kg fresh mussels
    • 2 onions, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
    • 2 leeks, cleaned and thinly sliced
    • 1 bunch of fresh parsley, chopped
    • 2 cups dry white wine
    • Butter
  • For the fries:
    • 4 large potatoes
    • Oil for frying
    • Salt

Instructions

  1. Rinse and debeard the mussels. Discard any that do not close when tapped.
  2. In a large pot, melt some butter and sauté the onions, garlic, and leeks until softened.
  3. Add the mussels, parsley, and white wine to the pot. Cover and cook on high heat, shaking the pot occasionally, until the mussels have opened (about 5-7 minutes).
  4. While the mussels are cooking, peel the potatoes and cut into fries. Rinse well, then dry thoroughly.
  5. Heat the oil in a deep fryer or large, deep saucepan to 160°C (320°F). Cook the fries for about 5 minutes, until soft but not yet coloring. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  6. Increase the oil temperature to 190°C (375°F). Add the fries back into the oil and cook for a further 2-3 minutes, until golden and crisp. Drain on paper towels and season with salt.
  7. Discard any mussels that did not open during cooking. Serve the mussels in bowls with the broth and fries on the side.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes

Enjoy your Moules-Frites!
