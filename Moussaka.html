Moussaka

Ingredients

  • 3 large aubergines, sliced
  • 500g minced beef
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • For the Bechamel sauce:
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 100g grated cheese (cheddar or Parmesan)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C. Lightly salt the aubergine slices and set aside for 30 minutes to extract the bitter juices. Rinse and pat dry.
  2. In a large pan, cook the onions, garlic, and minced beef until browned. Add the tomatoes, tomato paste, cinnamon, nutmeg, and parsley. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. In another pan, melt the butter and stir in the flour to make a roux. Gradually whisk in the milk until you have a smooth sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes, then remove from the heat and stir in the beaten eggs and half the cheese.
  4. Layer half the aubergine slices in an ovenproof dish, cover with the meat sauce, then arrange the rest of the aubergine on top. Pour over the Bechamel sauce, making sure it covers everything. Sprinkle with the rest of the cheese.
  5. Bake in the oven for 30-40 minutes until the top is golden and bubbling. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 70 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 40 minutes
