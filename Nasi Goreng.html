Nasi Goreng

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked jasmine rice
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 red chili, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp shrimp paste
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
  • 2 spring onions, sliced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken or shrimp (optional)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok or frying pan over medium heat. Add shallots, garlic, and chili. Cook until fragrant.
  2. Add the shrimp paste and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, and kecap manis. Stir fry, ensuring the rice is well coated with the sauces.
  4. Add the spring onions and cooked chicken or shrimp (if using). Stir well and cook for a couple of minutes.
  5. In a separate pan, fry the eggs to your liking.
  6. Serve the fried rice with the fried egg on top.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes
