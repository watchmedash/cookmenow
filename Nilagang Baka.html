Nilagang Baka

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef shank
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 2 large potatoes, quartered
  • 2 carrots, cut into large chunks
  • 1 large cabbage, quartered
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, add beef, onion, garlic, peppercorns, and enough water to cover the meat. Bring to a boil and skim off any scum that rises to the top.
  2. Reduce heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 2 hours or until the beef is tender.
  3. Add the potatoes and carrots to the pot. Continue to cook for another 10-15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the cabbage and cook for another 3-5 minutes.
  5. Season with fish sauce and salt according to your preference. Let it simmer for a couple more minutes.
  6. Turn off the heat and serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 2 hours 45 minutes

Enjoy your Nilagang Baka!
