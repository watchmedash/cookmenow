Okonomiyaki

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups finely shredded cabbage
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup pickled red ginger, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Okonomiyaki sauce, to taste
  • Japanese mayonnaise, to taste
  • 1/4 cup bonito flakes
  • 1/4 cup seaweed flakes

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and water until smooth. Add the eggs, cabbage, green onions, and red ginger; mix well.
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Pour the batter into the skillet and spread it out to form a large pancake. Cook for about 3-4 minutes, until the bottom is golden brown. Flip and cook the other side for another 3-4 minutes.
  3. Transfer the pancake to a plate and drizzle with okonomiyaki sauce and mayonnaise. Sprinkle with bonito flakes and seaweed flakes before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes
