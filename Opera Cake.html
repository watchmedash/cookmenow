Opera Cake

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup almond meal
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 4 oz bittersweet chocolate, melted
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

For the Coffee Buttercream:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee granules, dissolved in 2 tablespoons hot water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 8 oz bittersweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease and line a 12x16-inch jelly roll pan.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs until pale and fluffy. Add powdered sugar, almond meal, flour, and cocoa powder. Fold in melted chocolate and butter.
  3. Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until set.
  4. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on a wire rack.
  5. For the buttercream, beat together the butter, powdered sugar, coffee, and vanilla until smooth and creamy.
  6. Cut the cake into three equal rectangles. Place one layer on a serving platter, spread with half the buttercream, then top with the second layer. Spread with the remaining buttercream, then top with the third layer.
  7. For the ganache, heat the cream until simmering, then pour over the chocolate. Let sit for a minute, then stir until smooth. Spread over the top of the cake.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours to set before slicing and serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 40 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 55 minutes (plus cooling and chilling time)

Enjoy your Opera Cake!
