Orange Glazed Salmon

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 orange, zested and juiced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 green onions, sliced for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the salmon fillets and cook for 3-4 minutes per side until browned and cooked through. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, whisk together the orange zest, orange juice, soy sauce, honey, ginger, and garlic. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in the cornstarch mixture and continue to cook, stirring, until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Return the salmon to the pan and spoon the glaze over the top. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the salmon is heated through and coated with the glaze.
  6. Transfer to serving plates and garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes

Enjoy your Orange Glazed Salmon!
