Osso Buco

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces veal shanks
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped parsley
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest

Instructions

  1. Season veal shanks with salt and pepper, then dredge in flour, shaking off excess.
  2. In a large dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the veal shanks and brown on all sides. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Add the onion, celery, and carrot to the pan and cook until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. De-glaze the pan with the wine, scraping up any browned bits on the bottom of the pan.
  5. Add the tomatoes, beef broth, and bay leaf to the pan, then return the veal shanks to the pan. The liquid should come halfway up the sides of the shanks.
  6. Cover and simmer for about 2 hours, or until the meat is tender and falling off the bone.
  7. Mix the parsley and lemon zest together to make a gremolata. Sprinkle over the osso buco before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 2 hours
  • Total Time: 2 hours 20 minutes
