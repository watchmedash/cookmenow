Pad See Ew

Ingredients

  • 200g of wide rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 300g of chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup of gai lan (Chinese broccoli), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of granulated sugar
  • White pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water for about 20 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the garlic and stir-fry until it starts to golden.
  3. Add the chicken to the pan and stir-fry until it's no longer pink.
  4. Push the chicken to one side of the pan and pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Stir the eggs until they are scrambled, then mix them with the chicken.
  5. Add the noodles and gai lan to the pan, then add the oyster sauce, soy sauce, dark soy sauce, sugar, and a dash of white pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients and ensure the noodles and chicken are well coated with the sauce.
  6. Continue to stir-fry for a few more minutes until the noodles are tender and the gai lan is cooked.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes

Enjoy your Pad See Ew!
