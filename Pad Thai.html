Pad Thai

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Cilantro for garnish (optional)
  • For the sauce:
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)

Instructions

  1. Soak the rice noodles in lukewarm water for about 30 minutes until they are soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the fish sauce, tamarind paste, sugar, and chili powder to make the sauce. Set aside.
  3. Heat the oil in a large wok or pan over medium-high heat. Add the tofu and cook until golden brown. Push to one side of the wok.
  4. Crack the eggs into the wok. Scramble the eggs and cook until just set.
  5. Add the drained noodles to the wok. Pour the sauce over the noodles. Stir fry for a couple of minutes until the noodles are soft and coated with the sauce.
  6. Add the bean sprouts, peanuts, and green onions. Stir fry for another minute.
  7. Serve the Pad Thai hot with lime wedges and cilantro, if desired.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
