Paella

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1.5 cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup cooked and diced chicken
  • 1/2 cup cooked and diced shrimp
  • 1/2 cup mussels
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or paella pan over medium heat. Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic, cook until softened.
  2. Add rice to the skillet and stir to coat the rice in the oil. Add the chicken broth, saffron, paprika, thyme, and tomatoes, bring to a simmer and let it cook, without stirring, for 20 minutes.
  3. Add the peas, chicken, shrimp, and mussels. Cover and simmer for an additional 10 minutes, until the rice is cooked and the seafood is fully cooked.
  4. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve with lemon wedges.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour
