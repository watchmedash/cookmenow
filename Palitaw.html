Palitaw

Ingredients

  • 2 cups glutinous rice flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup sesame seeds, toasted

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the glutinous rice flour and water. Mix it well until a dough is formed.
  2. Take about 2 tablespoons of the dough and shape it into a ball. Flatten it using the palm of your hand.
  3. Repeat this process until all of the dough is used.
  4. Boil a pot of water. Once the water is boiling, drop the rice cakes into the pot. Cook them until they float to the surface.
  5. While waiting for the rice cakes to cook, combine the sugar and toasted sesame seeds in a separate bowl.
  6. Once the rice cakes float to the surface, remove them from the pot and drain the excess water.
  7. Dip each rice cake into the grated coconut, making sure it's fully covered.
  8. Finally, dip the rice cake into the sugar and sesame seeds mixture.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes

Enjoy your Palitaw!
