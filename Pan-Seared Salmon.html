Pan-Seared Salmon

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced

Instructions

  1. Season salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the salmon fillets, skin side down, and cook for about 5 minutes.
  4. Flip the salmon and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Remove the salmon from the pan and add the minced garlic to the pan.
  6. Stir in the lemon juice and cook for 1 minute.
  7. Pour the garlic-lemon sauce over the cooked salmon before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 5 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes
