Pancit Bihon

Ingredients

  • 200g bihon noodles (rice stick noodles)
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges, for serving
  • Spring onions, chopped for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak the bihon noodles in water until they become soft then drain. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add carrots and cabbage to the pan and cook for about 2 minutes.
  4. Add the noodles, chicken stock, and soy sauce to the pan. Stir well and cook until the noodles have absorbed the liquid and are cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with spring onions before serving.
  6. Serve with lemon wedges on the side.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
