Filipino Pancit Canton

Ingredients

  • 250 grams Pancit Canton noodles
  • 1 chicken breast, boiled and shredded
  • 1/2 cup pork, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shrimps, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, chopped
  • 1/2 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup snow peas (sitsaro)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 5 cups chicken broth
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 stalks green onions for garnish
  • 2 pieces calamansi, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok. Saut√© the garlic and onion.
  2. Add the pork and cook until lightly browned. Add the shredded chicken and shrimps, cook until shrimps turn pink.
  3. Add the carrots, bell pepper, cabbage, and snow peas. Stir fry for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add the chicken broth, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Bring to a boil.
  5. Add the Pancit Canton noodles, gently pushing them down to soak up the broth. Cook until the noodles are tender and have absorbed most of the broth.
  6. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if necessary.
  7. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with green onions and calamansi on the side.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes
