Pancit Malabon

Ingredients

  • 500g Malabon noodles or thick rice noodles
  • 1 cup shrimp, peeled and cooked
  • 1 cup squid, cleaned and sliced
  • 1 cup tinapa flakes (smoked fish flakes)
  • 2 cups pork belly, boiled and sliced
  • 4 cups annatto water (water mixed with annatto seeds)
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup fish sauce
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • 1 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 cup crushed chicharon (pork rinds)
  • 1/2 cup oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat oil over medium heat. Saute garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add flour and cook until it forms a roux.
  4. Gradually add the annatto water while stirring continuously until well combined.
  5. Add fish sauce and continue stirring until sauce thickens.
  6. Add the cooked shrimp, squid, tinapa flakes, and pork. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Combine the sauce with the noodles, mixing thoroughly until all noodles are coated with sauce.
  8. Top with slices of hard-boiled eggs, green onions, and crushed chicharon before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour

Enjoy your Pancit Malabon!
