Pancit Palabok

Ingredients

  • 250g rice noodles
  • 4 tablespoons oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 200g ground pork
  • 1 cup tinapa flakes (smoked fish)
  • 2 cups shrimp broth
  • 1/4 cup annatto water (made by soaking annatto seeds in water)
  • 1/2 cup flour, dissolved in water
  • 1/2 cup crushed chicharon (pork rinds)
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cooked shrimp
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • Fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the rice noodles until cooked, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© garlic and onion until softened.
  3. Add ground pork and tinapa flakes, cook until browned.
  4. Add shrimp broth and annatto water, bring to a boil.
  5. Add dissolved flour to the pan and stir until the sauce thickens.
  6. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
  7. Arrange the cooked noodles on a plate, pour the sauce over the noodles.
  8. Top with chicharon, sliced eggs, shrimp, and chopped spring onions. Serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 35 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes

Enjoy your Pancit Palabok!
