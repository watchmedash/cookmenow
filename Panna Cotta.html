Panna Cotta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean or 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons unflavored gelatin
  • 2 tablespoons cold water
  • Fresh berries, for garnish
  • Mint leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, sprinkle the gelatin over cold water and let it stand for 5-10 minutes to soften.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla (if using a vanilla bean, split and scrape the seeds into the pan). Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar is dissolved and the mixture is hot but not boiling.
  3. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the softened gelatin until completely dissolved.
  4. If using vanilla extract instead of a vanilla bean, stir it in at this point.
  5. Divide the mixture among six serving glasses or ramekins and let cool to room temperature. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.
  6. To serve, garnish with fresh berries and mint leaves.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Chilling Time: 4 hours
  • Total Time: 4 hours 25 minutes

Enjoy your Panna Cotta!
