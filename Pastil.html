Pastil

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 200g shredded cooked chicken (or other meat like beef or fish)
  • 1/4 cup coconut vinegar or regular white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Banana leaves (for wrapping)

Instructions

  1. Wash the glutinous rice thoroughly until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, combine the washed glutinous rice and water. Let it soak for at least 1 hour.
  3. After soaking, drain any excess water from the rice.
  4. Prepare your steamer. Line it with banana leaves to prevent sticking.
  5. Place the drained glutinous rice on the banana leaves and spread it out evenly.
  6. Steam the rice over medium heat for about 20-25 minutes or until fully cooked and tender.
  7. While the rice is steaming, prepare the shredded cooked chicken (or other meat) by seasoning it with salt and vinegar. Mix well and set aside.
  8. Once the rice is cooked, remove it from the steamer and let it cool slightly.
  9. Take a portion of the cooked rice and flatten it on a piece of banana leaf.
  10. Add a generous amount of the seasoned shredded chicken (or other meat) on top of the rice.
  11. Wrap the banana leaf around the rice and meat to form a triangular shape, similar to a mini-packet.
  12. Serve the Pastil while still warm and wrapped in the banana leaf. Enjoy this traditional and flavorful Filipino dish!

Time

  • Preparation Time: 1 hour (including soaking time)
  • Cooking Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour and 25 minutes

Discover the unique and delicious flavors of Pastil, a traditional Filipino dish of steamed glutinous rice topped with seasoned shredded chicken, wrapped in banana leaves!
