Pav Bhaji

Ingredients

  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 8 pav buns
  • Chopped onion and lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Boil the potatoes, cauliflower, carrots, and peas until tender. Drain and mash the vegetables together; set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until it starts to brown. Stir in the pav bhaji masala, turmeric, and chili powder.
  3. Add the tomatoes and cook until they become soft. Add the mashed vegetables and mix well. Cook for another 5-10 minutes.
  4. Melt the butter in a separate pan and lightly toast the pav buns on both sides.
  5. Serve the bhaji with the toasted buns, chopped onion, and a squeeze of lemon.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes
