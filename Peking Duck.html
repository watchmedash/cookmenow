Peking Duck

Ingredients

  • 1 whole duck
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon Chinese five-spice powder
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 10 Chinese pancakes
  • Hoisin sauce
  • 6 green onions, cut into long thin strips
  • 1 cucumber, cut into long thin strips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Rinse the duck inside and out, and pat dry.
  2. Mix together the honey, five-spice, soy sauce, white pepper, and salt. Brush the mixture all over the duck, both inside and out. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours, or overnight in the fridge for best results.
  3. Roast the duck in the preheated oven for about 1 hour or until the skin is crispy and golden brown. You may need to drain the fat from the roasting pan halfway through.
  4. While the duck is roasting, prepare the pancakes according to the package instructions.
  5. Once the duck is done, let it rest for about 10 minutes, then carve it into thin slices.
  6. Serve the duck slices with the pancakes, hoisin sauce, green onions, and cucumber. Each person can make their own pancake wrap with the ingredients.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 3 hours 30 minutes
