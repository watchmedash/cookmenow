Penne Alla Vodka

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vodka
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Red pepper flakes, to taste
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Chopped fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain, reserving 1 cup of the pasta water, and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat the olive oil. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the vodka to the skillet and let it simmer for 4-5 minutes to reduce slightly. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and cook for another 5-7 minutes to let the flavors meld together.
  4. Lower the heat and stir in the heavy cream, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Cook for a couple more minutes until the sauce is heated through.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add some of the reserved pasta water.
  6. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until it's melted and the pasta is well coated. Check for seasoning and adjust as needed.
  7. Serve the pasta in bowls, garnished with fresh basil.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes

Enjoy your Penne Alla Vodka!
