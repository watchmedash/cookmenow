Phở Gà (Vietnamese Chicken Noodle Soup)

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 onion, unpeeled and cut in half
  • 2 liters of water
  • 1 piece of ginger, unpeeled
  • 1 package pho spices (star anise, cloves, coriander seeds, cardamom)
  • Fresh rice noodles
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • 1 bunch cilantro, chopped
  • Fresh bean sprouts
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Fresh Thai basil
  • Hoisin sauce, sriracha (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Char onion and ginger over an open flame (either on a grill or gas stove). You can skip this step if you do not have a gas stove.
  2. In a large pot, add water, chicken, charred onion and ginger, and pho spices. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer.
  3. Skim off any foam and impurities that float to the top. Simmer for 1 hour.
  4. Remove chicken from pot, separate the meat from the bones, and return the bones to the pot. Continue simmering the broth for another hour.
  5. Strain the broth and season with salt. Return the broth to the pot and keep hot.
  6. Prepare the fresh noodles according to the package instructions.
  7. To serve, place some chicken meat and noodles in a bowl, ladle hot broth over, and top with spring onions and cilantro. Serve with a plate of bean sprouts, lime wedges, and Thai basil on the side. Add hoisin sauce and sriracha to taste.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 2 hours
  • Total Time: 2 hours 20 minutes

Enjoy your Phở Gà!
