Pinakbet

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 1 large bitter melon, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sliced okra
  • 1 cup squash, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 1 cup long beans, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 1 cup string beans, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© onions, garlic, and tomatoes until soft.
  2. Add shrimp paste to the pan and continue to cook for about 2 minutes.
  3. Add all the vegetables to the pan and stir well. Cover and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until all the vegetables are cooked.
  4. Stir occasionally to prevent the vegetables from sticking to the pan.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes
