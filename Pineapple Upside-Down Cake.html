Pineapple Upside-Down Cake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 can (20 oz) pineapple slices, drained
  • 10 maraschino cherries, halved

For the Cake Batter:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Pour the melted butter into a 9-inch round cake pan, and sprinkle with brown sugar.
  2. Arrange the pineapple slices over the sugar, and place a cherry half in the center of each pineapple ring.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  4. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter, milk, eggs, and vanilla until smooth. Gradually add the dry ingredients, mixing just until combined.
  5. Pour the batter over the pineapple slices, spreading evenly.
  6. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Immediately invert onto a serving platter, leaving the pan over the cake for a few minutes to let the sugar glaze drip down.
  8. Cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
  • Total Time: 65 minutes

Enjoy your Pineapple Upside-Down Cake!
