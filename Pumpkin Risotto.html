Pumpkin Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth, kept warm
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh sage leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until soft and translucent.
  2. Add the Arborio rice to the skillet, stirring to coat with the butter. Cook for a couple of minutes until the edges of the grains become translucent.
  3. Pour in the white wine, and stir until mostly evaporated.
  4. Begin adding the warm broth one ladleful at a time, stirring frequently. Wait until the liquid is mostly absorbed before adding the next ladle of broth.
  5. Continue this process until the rice is creamy and cooked to your preferred level of doneness, which should take about 18-20 minutes.
  6. Stir in the pumpkin puree, Parmesan cheese, nutmeg, salt, and pepper, cooking for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat, garnish with chopped fresh sage, and serve immediately.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes

Enjoy your Pumpkin Risotto!
