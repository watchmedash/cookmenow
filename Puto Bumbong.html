Puto Bumbong

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous rice (malagkit)
  • 2 teaspoons purple food color (ube)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup muscovado sugar
  • Grated coconut
  • Margarine or butter

Instructions

  1. Soak the glutinous rice in water overnight. Then, grind the rice and mix in the purple food coloring.
  2. Put the mixture into a bumbong (bamboo tube) and steam it in a large pot with enough water. Cover the pot and wait for the steam to cook the rice. You'll know it's done when the steam exits the bamboo tube.
  3. Remove the bamboo tube using a cloth to protect your hand. Hold the tube on a solid surface and push the puto bumbong out using a knife.
  4. Spread margarine or butter on top of the puto bumbong and sprinkle muscovado sugar and grated coconut on top.
  5. Repeat the process with the remaining mixture.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
