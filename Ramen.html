Ramen

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of Ramen Noodles
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • 1/2 cup sliced bamboo shoots
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • 1/2 cup sliced cooked pork
  • 1 sheet of nori (seaweed), cut into strips

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, add chicken broth, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and minced garlic. Bring to a boil.
  2. Add ramen noodles to the pot and cook as per the instructions on the packet.
  3. Divide the cooked noodles into two large bowls. Pour the soup on top.
  4. Top with sliced green onions, bamboo shoots, boiled egg halves, sliced pork, and strips of nori.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes
