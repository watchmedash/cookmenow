Ratatouille

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 6 Roma tomatoes
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 2 zucchini
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chopped thyme leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Cut vegetables into thin slices, set aside.
  3. In a large baking dish, spread garlic and drizzle with 1/2 the olive oil.
  4. Arrange slices of vegetables in the baking dish, alternating by type and overlapping them. Season with salt, pepper, thyme.
  5. Drizzle with remaining olive oil and cover the dish with foil.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes, until vegetables are tender and moist.
  7. Uncover and bake for another 15 minutes. Then sprinkle with fresh basil before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 1 hour 20 minutes
