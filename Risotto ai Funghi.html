Risotto ai Funghi

Ingredients

  • 1 litre chicken or vegetable stock
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1.5 cups Arborio rice
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 cup fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Bring the stock to a simmer in a saucepan.
  2. Heat the olive oil over medium heat in a separate, large pan. Add the onions and garlic, cooking until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add the Arborio rice to the pan and stir until each grain is coated with oil. Toast the rice for a few minutes until it starts to become translucent.
  4. Add the white wine to the rice, stirring constantly until it is fully absorbed.
  5. Start adding the simmering stock to the rice, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously. Allow each ladle of stock to be absorbed before adding the next. This process should take about 20 minutes.
  6. When the rice is al dente (cooked to be firm to the bite), add the sliced mushrooms, butter, and Parmesan cheese. Stir until the cheese and butter are melted and the mushrooms are heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and let the risotto rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 35 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes

Enjoy your Risotto ai Funghi!
