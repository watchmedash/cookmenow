Roasted Asparagus and Feta Tart

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Roll out the puff pastry on a floured surface into a rectangle, then transfer to the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Score a border around the edge of the pastry with a sharp knife, about 1 inch from the edge.
  4. Prick the inside of the border all over with a fork. Brush the border with the beaten egg.
  5. Toss the asparagus with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Arrange the asparagus inside the border of the puff pastry.
  6. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the asparagus.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown and the asparagus is tender.
  8. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with lemon zest and fresh dill.
  9. Allow to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes

Enjoy your Roasted Asparagus and Feta Tart!
