Roasted Beetroot and Goat Cheese Salad

Ingredients

  • 6 medium beetroots
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 100 grams of goat cheese
  • 50 grams of mixed salad greens
  • 1/4 cup of balsamic vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Peel and cut the beetroots into chunks, place them on a baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roast the beetroots in the oven for about 45 minutes, or until tender.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the roasted beetroots, mixed salad greens, and crumbled goat cheese.
  5. Drizzle the salad with balsamic vinegar before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour
