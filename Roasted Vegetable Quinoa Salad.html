Roasted Vegetable Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked quinoa
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F (200C).
  2. Place the chopped vegetables on a baking sheet. Drizzle with 2 tablespoons of olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa and roasted vegetables. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Stir until well combined.
  4. Sprinkle the salad with crumbled feta cheese before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes
