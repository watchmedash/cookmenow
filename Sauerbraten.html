Sauerbraten (German Pot Roast)

Ingredients

  • 1.5kg of beef roast
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 cup of red wine vinegar
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 large carrot, chopped
  • 1 stalk of celery, chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 6 cloves
  • 12 peppercorns
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup of flour
  • 1/2 cup of crushed ginger snap cookies (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the water, vinegar, onion, carrot, celery, bay leaves, cloves, peppercorns, sugar, and salt. Bring to a boil and then remove from heat. Allow to cool.
  2. Place the roast in a deep dish and pour the cooled marinade over it. Cover and refrigerate for 3-5 days, turning the meat daily.
  3. After marinating, remove the meat from the marinade and pat dry. Strain the marinade and set aside, reserving the vegetables and spices.
  4. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C).
  5. In a large ovenproof pot or Dutch oven, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the beef and brown on all sides.
  6. Remove the beef and set aside. Add the reserved vegetables and spices to the pot and cook until lightly browned. Sprinkle in the flour and stir well to combine.
  7. Gradually add the strained marinade, stirring constantly. Return the beef to the pot.
  8. Cover the pot and place it in the preheated oven. Cook for about 3 hours or until the beef is tender, turning the meat every 30 minutes.
  9. If desired, add crushed ginger snaps to the pot during the last half hour of cooking to thicken and sweeten the gravy.
  10. Once done, remove the beef and slice. Strain the gravy and serve it over the sliced beef.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Marinating Time: 72 hours
  • Cooking Time: 3 hours
  • Total Time: 76 hours 20 minutes

Enjoy your Sauerbraten!
