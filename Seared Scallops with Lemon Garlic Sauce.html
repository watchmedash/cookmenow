Seared Scallops with Lemon Garlic Sauce

Ingredients

  • 12 large sea scallops, patted dry
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until hot.
  2. Season scallops with salt and pepper and place them in the skillet. Cook for about 1-2 minutes on each side until golden brown and just cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the white wine and lemon juice, and bring to a simmer. Cook for 3-4 minutes until the sauce has slightly reduced.
  5. Return the scallops to the skillet, spooning the sauce over them, and cook for another minute until heated through.
  6. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve immediately.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 20 minutes

Enjoy your Seared Scallops with Lemon Garlic Sauce!
