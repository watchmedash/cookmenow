Shakshuka

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 large red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 large eggs
  • Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and bell pepper to the pan and saut√© until soft.
  3. Add the garlic, cumin, paprika, and cayenne to the pan and stir well.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes to the pan and season with salt and pepper. Let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Make wells in the tomato mixture and crack an egg into each well. Cover the pan and let the eggs cook to your desired level of doneness.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
