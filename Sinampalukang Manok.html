Sinampalukang Manok

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, julienned
  • 1 packet sinigang sa sampalok mix
  • 1 bundle tamarind leaves, washed
  • 1 bundle chili leaves or kangkong leaves
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 6 to 8 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil and saut√© the garlic, onion, and ginger.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add the fish sauce and mix well.
  4. Pour in the water and bring to a boil.
  5. Lower the heat and add the tamarind leaves and sinigang mix. Let it simmer until the chicken is cooked.
  6. Add the chili leaves or kangkong leaves and simmer for another 2 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 60 minutes
