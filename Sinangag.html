Sinangag

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of leftover rice
  • 10 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 spring onions, chopped (optional)
  • 2 eggs, beaten (optional)

Instructions

  1. Break up the clumps of your leftover rice to ensure it's loose. Set it aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large frying pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic to the pan. Stir continuously until it turns golden brown. Be careful not to burn it.
  4. If you're adding eggs, push the garlic to one side of the pan. Pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs and cook until set.
  5. Add the rice to the pan. Stir everything together, making sure the garlic and eggs are evenly distributed.
  6. Season with salt and stir again. Continue to cook for a few more minutes until the rice is heated through.
  7. Optional: garnish with chopped spring onions before serving.
  8. Serve hot as a side dish with your favorite main dish.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes

Enjoy your Sinangag!
