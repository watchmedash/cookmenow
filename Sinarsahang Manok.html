Sinarsahang Manok

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 pieces green chili peppers
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onion and garlic until softened.
  2. Add the chicken pieces, cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add green chili peppers and coconut milk. Bring to a boil then simmer for about 30 minutes, or until chicken is cooked and sauce has thickened.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes

Enjoy your Sinarsahang Manok!
