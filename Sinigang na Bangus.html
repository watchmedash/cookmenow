Sinigang na Bangus

Ingredients

  • 1 whole bangus (milkfish), cleaned and sliced into serving pieces
  • 1 packet of sinigang mix
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 bunch of kangkong (water spinach), leaves and tender stalks only
  • 1 radish, sliced
  • 2 long green peppers
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 6 cups of water
  • Fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Saut√© the onions until translucent, then add the tomatoes and cook until softened.
  2. Add the radish slices and cook for a couple of minutes.
  3. Add the bangus slices to the pot and cook each side for about 3 minutes.
  4. Pour in the water and bring to a boil. Skim off any scum that floats to the surface.
  5. Stir in the sinigang mix until fully dissolved. Add the long green peppers and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the kangkong leaves and simmer for another 2 minutes.
  7. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper according to your taste. Let it simmer for another minute.
  8. Turn off the heat. Serve the sinigang hot with a side of steamed rice. Enjoy!

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
