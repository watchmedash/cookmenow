Souvlaki

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless chicken, pork, or lamb, cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Pita bread or flatbread
  • Fresh vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, onions, etc.)
  • Tzatziki sauce or your favorite dipping sauce

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  2. Add the cubed chicken, pork, or lamb to the marinade and toss to coat. Cover the bowl and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or ideally overnight for maximum flavor.
  3. Preheat the grill or stovetop grill pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated meat onto skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 4-5 minutes on each side or until the meat is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Warm the pita bread or flatbread on the grill for a minute on each side.
  7. Assemble the souvlaki by placing the grilled meat on the warm pita bread.
  8. Add fresh vegetables and your favorite dipping sauce, such as tzatziki, on top.
  9. Roll up the pita bread to enclose the filling, and serve the souvlaki warm.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes (plus marinating time)
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes (plus marinating time)

Facts about Souvlaki

  • Souvlaki is a popular Greek fast food that consists of small pieces of grilled meat on a skewer.
  • The meat used in souvlaki can vary and is often made with chicken, pork, or lamb.
  • The marinade is a key component of souvlaki, typically made with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and various herbs and spices, giving the meat its delicious flavor.
  • Souvlaki is a versatile dish that can be served as a main course or as part of a mezze platter with various side dishes.
  • It is commonly served in a warm pita bread or flatbread, along with fresh vegetables and a flavorful dipping sauce like tzatziki.
  • Souvlaki is a popular street food in Greece and is enjoyed by locals and tourists alike.
  • It is a quick and easy dish to prepare, making it a great option for weeknight dinners or outdoor grilling parties.
  • The combination of tender, juicy meat, aromatic herbs, and tangy tzatziki sauce makes souvlaki a delicious and satisfying meal.
  • With its simple yet flavorful ingredients, souvlaki is a delightful taste of Greek cuisine that can be enjoyed by everyone.
