Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Slice the garlic and saut√© in olive oil until lightly golden.
  3. Add the red pepper flakes to the garlic and oil.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the garlic and oil. Toss to coat.
  5. Season with salt and garnish with parsley.
