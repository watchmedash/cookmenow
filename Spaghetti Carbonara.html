Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until tender but still al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, fry the pancetta in a hot pan until crispy, then set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and mix in half of the cheese.
  4. Once the spaghetti is done, reserve some of the water and drain the rest.
  5. Add the beaten eggs, cooking until the eggs are slightly scrambled, then add the pancetta.
  6. Add a bit of the reserved water if the sauce is too thick, then mix in the rest of the cheese.
  7. Season with a generous amount of black pepper, then serve immediately.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
