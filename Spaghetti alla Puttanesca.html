Spaghetti alla Puttanesca

Ingredients

  • 400 grams spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 6 anchovy fillets, chopped
  • 1 400-gram can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup black olives, pitted and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium heat, heat the olive oil. Add garlic, red pepper flakes, and anchovies. Saut√© until the garlic is golden and the anchovies have melted into the oil, about 3 minutes.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes to the skillet. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes have thickened slightly, about 10 minutes.
  4. Add olives and capers to the skillet, and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Drain the spaghetti, reserving 1 cup of the pasta water. Add the spaghetti to the skillet, along with a little pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce. Toss until the spaghetti is fully coated in the sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and stir in the chopped parsley.
  7. Transfer to a serving dish, garnish with more parsley if desired, and serve immediately.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes

Enjoy your Spaghetti alla Puttanesca!
