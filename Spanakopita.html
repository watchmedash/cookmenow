Spanakopita

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1 pound spinach, washed and drained
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 14 sheets phyllo dough

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Saut√© onions until soft and lightly browned.
  3. Add spinach to the pan and cook until spinach wilts and the liquid is evaporated. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
  4. In a large bowl, combine feta, eggs, salt, and pepper. Add the cooled spinach and onions to the bowl and mix until combined.
  5. Brush the bottom of a baking dish with melted butter. Lay a sheet of phyllo dough in the dish, and brush with butter. Repeat this process with 7 more sheets of phyllo.
  6. Spread the spinach and cheese mixture over the phyllo dough. Cover with the remaining sheets of phyllo, brushing each with butter. Tuck in any overhanging edges.
  7. Use a sharp knife to cut the spanakopita into squares or triangles. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes, until golden brown.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour

Enjoy your Spanakopita!
