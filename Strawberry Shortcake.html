Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 5 tablespoons granulated sugar, divided
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 2/3 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the strawberries with 2 tablespoons of sugar. Stir and set aside for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, 1/4 cup sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or fork until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Stir in the milk and vanilla extract until just combined. Drop by large spoonfuls onto a greased baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. In a chilled bowl, beat the heavy cream with the remaining 3 tablespoons of sugar until stiff peaks form.
  7. To serve, split the shortcakes in half horizontally. Spoon the strawberries onto the bottom halves, top with whipped cream, and replace the top halves of the shortcakes.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes (plus time for strawberries to macerate)

Enjoy your Strawberry Shortcake!
