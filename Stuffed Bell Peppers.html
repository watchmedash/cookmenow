Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients

  • 4 bell peppers, any color
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Remove the tops, seeds, and membranes of the bell peppers. Arrange peppers in a baking dish with the hollowed sides facing upward.
  3. In a skillet over medium heat, heat olive oil and add onions. Cook until onions are translucent, about 5 minutes. Add in ground beef, and cook until the beef is fully cooked.
  4. Stir in the rice, diced tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Cook and stir until heated through. Remove from heat, and stir in the cheese.
  5. Stuff the mixture into the prepared bell peppers, and place them back into the baking dish. Cover with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven, covered, for 25 minutes. Then remove the aluminum foil, and bake for an additional 20 minutes.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 5 minutes

Enjoy your Stuffed Bell Peppers!
