Sushi Roll

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice, cooked
  • 10 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 200g fresh sushi-grade salmon, thinly sliced
  • Soy sauce, for serving
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger, for serving

Instructions

  1. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice on top of the nori.
  2. Arrange cucumber, avocado and salmon slices on the rice. Roll it up tightly using the mat, pressing firmly to compact everything.
  3. Use a sharp knife to slice the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  5. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi and pickled ginger.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
