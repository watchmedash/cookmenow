Swedish Meatballs

Ingredients

  • For the Meatballs:
    • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 1 pound ground beef
    • 1/2 pound ground pork
    • 1 large egg
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • For the Sauce:
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    • 2 cups beef broth
    • 1 cup sour cream
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak breadcrumbs in milk until softened, about 10 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, mix soaked breadcrumbs, ground beef, ground pork, egg, garlic powder, salt, pepper, allspice, and nutmeg until well combined.
  3. Shape mixture into small, round meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. Place on a tray.
  4. Preheat a large skillet over medium heat. Add meatballs and cook, turning occasionally, until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Transfer meatballs to a plate.
  5. In the same skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Sprinkle in flour, and whisk to incorporate. Cook until flour turns golden brown, about 2 minutes.
  6. Slowly whisk in beef broth, and bring to a simmer. Cook until sauce thickens, about 2 minutes. Lower the heat, stir in sour cream, and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Add meatballs back into the skillet, and simmer until heated through, about 2 minutes. Sprinkle with parsley before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes

Enjoy your Swedish Meatballs!
