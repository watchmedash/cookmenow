Tapsilog

Ingredients

  • 500 grams beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups cooked garlic rice
  • 2 fried eggs
  • Pickled papaya (atsara), to serve

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the beef, garlic, soy sauce, sugar, salt, and pepper. Mix well and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat. Add the beef and marinade, cook until the beef is browned and the marinade has reduced.
  3. Serve the beef with garlic rice and a fried egg. Add a side of atsara if desired.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Marinating Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 60 minutes

Enjoy your Tapsilog!
