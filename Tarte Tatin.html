Tarte Tatin

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 large Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Melt butter in a 9-inch oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Stir in sugar and vanilla extract until sugar starts to dissolve, 2 to 3 minutes. Add apple slices and cook until apples are starting to soften and caramel is brown, 10 to 15 minutes. Remove from heat.
  3. Roll out puff pastry on a floured work surface and place over apples, tucking edges into the skillet. Brush pastry with beaten egg.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until pastry is puffed and golden brown, about 25 minutes. Let cool in the skillet, about 5 minutes.
  5. Carefully invert onto a serving plate. Cut into slices and serve warm or at room temperature.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour

Enjoy your Tarte Tatin!
