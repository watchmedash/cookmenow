Tempura

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup iced water
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour, sifted
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 sweet potato, sliced into thin rounds
  • 1 Japanese eggplant, sliced into thin rounds
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced into thin strips
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, lightly beat the egg. Add the iced water to the bowl and mix.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the sifted flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Gradually add the flour mixture to the egg and water, mixing lightly with chopsticks. Be careful not to overmix, it should be a little lumpy.
  4. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep pot over medium-high heat.
  5. Dip the shrimp and vegetables into the batter, allowing any excess to drip off.
  6. Carefully place the battered shrimp and vegetables into the hot oil, working in batches. Fry until golden brown, turning once, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Use a slotted spoon to remove the tempura from the oil, draining on paper towels and sprinkling with a little salt while still hot.
  8. Serve the tempura immediately with a dipping sauce of your choice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes

Enjoy your Tempura!
