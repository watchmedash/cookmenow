Thai Green Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons Thai green curry paste
  • 400ml can coconut milk
  • 500g boneless chicken pieces
  • 1 cup diced aubergine
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large wok or pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, cook until softened.
  2. Add the curry paste, stirring until fragrant.
  3. Add the coconut milk, chicken, aubergine, and bell pepper. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat and simmer, uncovered, for about 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  5. Stir in the fish sauce, sugar, and basil. Cook for a further 2 minutes.
  6. Serve the curry hot with steamed jasmine rice.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes
