Tom Kha Gai

Ingredients

  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1.5 pounds chicken breast, cut into thin strips
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, cut into 3-inch pieces and smashed
  • 4 slices galangal
  • 6 kaffir lime leaves, torn into pieces
  • 4 Thai bird chilis, smashed
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 200g oyster mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pot, combine coconut milk, chicken broth, lemongrass, galangal, lime leaves, and bird chilis. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken, onion, tomatoes, and mushrooms. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until the chicken is cooked and the flavors are well combined, about 20 minutes.
  3. Add the fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice. Stir well to combine.
  4. Remove from heat and discard the lemongrass, galangal, lime leaves, and chilis. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with cilantro leaves before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes
