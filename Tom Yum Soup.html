Tom Yum Soup

Ingredients

  • 1.5 litres chicken stock
  • 4 kaffir lime leaves, torn into pieces
  • 3 stalks lemongrass, cut into 1-inch pieces and crushed
  • 5 slices galangal
  • 5 bird's eye chilies, crushed
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 300g prawns/shrimp, peeled and de-veined
  • 200g straw mushrooms or other fresh mushrooms, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 small white onion, quartered
  • 2-3 limes, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, add the chicken stock, kaffir lime leaves, lemongrass, galangal, bird's eye chilies, and garlic. Bring to a boil and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Add the prawns, mushrooms, tomatoes, and onion to the pot. Continue to cook until the prawns turn pink. This should take about 5-10 minutes.
  3. Season the soup with fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar. Adjust according to your preference. Some like it more sour, while others prefer a more salty or sweet taste.
  4. Once done, turn off the heat. Ladle the soup into serving bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes

Enjoy your Tom Yum Soup!
