Tres Leches Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 5 large eggs, separated
  • 1 cup granulated sugar, divided
  • 1/3 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 12 fluid ounce-can evaporated milk
  • 14 ounce-can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9x13 inch baking pan.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt together and set aside.
  3. Beat egg yolks with 3/4 cup sugar on high speed until yolks are pale yellow. Stir in milk and vanilla. Pour egg yolk mixture over the flour mixture and combine gently.
  4. Beat egg whites on high speed until soft peaks form. Add the remaining 1/4 cup sugar gradually and continue beating until egg whites are firm but not dry.
  5. Fold egg white mixture into the batter gently, just until combined. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Allow cake to cool.
  7. Combine the evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk, and 1/4 cup of the whole milk in a small pitcher. Once the cake is cool, pierce it in several places using a fork and gradually drizzle all but about 1 cup of the milk mixtureâ€”trying to get as much around the edges of the cake as you can.
  8. Allow the cake to absorb the milk mixture for 30 minutes, then whip the heavy cream with the powdered sugar and the remaining 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract until thick and spreadable.
  9. Spread a thin layer of whipped cream over the surface of the cake (use more if you wish) and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour
