Turon (Filipino Banana Spring Rolls)

Ingredients

  • 6 pieces saba bananas (or plantains)
  • 12 pieces spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup cooking oil
  • 1 cup jackfruit, sliced (optional)

Instructions

  1. Peel the bananas and cut in half lengthwise. Set aside.
  2. Lay a spring roll wrapper on a flat surface. Dip a banana half into brown sugar and lay it across one end of the wrapper. Add some slices of jackfruit on top of the banana, if desired.
  3. Roll the wrapper around the banana, folding in the edges as you go. Wet the end of the wrapper with water to seal the spring roll.
  4. Repeat with the remaining bananas and wrappers.
  5. Heat the oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the spring rolls and fry until golden brown, turning occasionally for even cooking.
  6. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy!

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes
