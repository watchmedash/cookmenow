Tuyo with Garlic Rice and Fried Egg

Ingredients

  • 8 pieces of tuyo (salted dried fish)
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of leftover cooked rice
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of vinegar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons of cooking oil, divided

Instructions

  1. Fry the tuyo in 2 tablespoons of oil until crispy. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add 1 more tablespoon of oil. Sauté the minced garlic until golden brown. Remove some for garnishing.
  3. Add the rice to the pan with the sautéed garlic. Stir fry until all the grains are coated with oil and the garlic is evenly distributed.
  4. In a separate pan, heat the remaining tablespoon of oil and fry the eggs. Season with a little salt and pepper.
  5. Arrange a serving of garlic rice, fried tuyo, and fried egg on a plate. Sprinkle the fried egg with the reserved fried garlic. Drizzle the tuyo with a bit of vinegar if you like.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes

Enjoy your Tuyo with Garlic Rice and Fried Egg!
