Vanilla Bean Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 vanilla bean, seeds scraped out
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese, sugar, vanilla extract, and vanilla bean seeds until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add the sour cream, flour, and milk, and beat until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter over the crust in the pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until the center is set and the top is lightly browned. Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove the sides of the pan and cool completely on a wire rack.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 60 minutes
  • Total Time: 80 minutes (plus chilling time)

Enjoy your Vanilla Bean Cheesecake!
