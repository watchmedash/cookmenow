Vegan Black Bean Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Fresh coriander, for garnish
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions and bell peppers, and saut√© until they are soft and starting to caramelize.
  2. Add the black beans, cumin, chili powder, salt and pepper to the pan. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet over medium-high heat for about 30 seconds on each side.
  4. Fill each tortilla with the black bean mixture, a few slices of avocado, and a sprinkling of fresh coriander. Serve with a wedge of lime on the side.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes
