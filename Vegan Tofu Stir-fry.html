Vegan Tofu Stir-fry

Ingredients

  • 200g firm tofu
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions

  1. Press the tofu to remove excess water, then cut into cubes.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of the vegetable oil in a large frying pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Add the tofu to the pan and fry until golden on all sides. Remove the tofu from the pan and set aside.
  4. Add the remaining oil to the pan, then add the sliced peppers, minced garlic, and ginger. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the peppers are tender.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, cornstarch, and water. Pour this sauce over the stir-fried vegetables in the pan.
  6. Add the fried tofu back into the pan and stir well to coat everything in the sauce.
  7. Cook for a few more minutes until the sauce has thickened. Serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes
