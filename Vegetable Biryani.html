Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 bell peppers, chopped
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Wash the Basmati rice under cold running water until the water runs clear. Soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan. Add onions and cook until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add the chopped vegetables, green peas, biryani masala, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  4. Drain the rice and add it to the pan. Add water and stir gently to mix. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat, cover the pan, and cook for 15-20 minutes until the rice is cooked and all the water is absorbed.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes
