Vegetable Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped zucchini
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara pasta sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir peppers, zucchini, onions, and garlic in oil until tender. Mix in the cooked noodles, ricotta cheese, basil, and egg. Layer 1/2 of the lasagna noodles in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover noodles with 1/2 of the vegetable mixture, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, and 1 cup marinara sauce. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 40 minutes.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes
