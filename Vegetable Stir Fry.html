Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and onion and stir fry for a minute.
  3. Add bell peppers, broccoli, mushrooms, and stir fry for another 5 minutes.
  4. Add spinach, soy sauce, oyster sauce and stir well. Cook for another 2 minutes until the spinach is wilted.
  5. Serve hot with rice or noodles.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 20 minutes
