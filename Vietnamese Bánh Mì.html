Vietnamese Bánh Mì

Ingredients

  • 1 French baguette
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 2 sprigs cilantro
  • 1 jalapeno, sliced
  • Mayonnaise, to spread
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix soy sauce, fish sauce, honey, and minced garlic in a bowl. Add chicken breasts and marinate for at least an hour.
  2. Grill the marinated chicken until fully cooked and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  3. Split the baguette into halves, spread a layer of mayonnaise, and begin to layer your sandwich. Start with the sliced chicken, followed by the julienned carrot, cucumber, cilantro, and jalapeno slices. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Close your sandwich and cut it into your desired portions.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes (plus marination time)
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes (plus marination time)

Enjoy your Vietnamese Bánh Mì!
