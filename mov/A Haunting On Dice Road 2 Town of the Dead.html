A Haunting On Dice Road 2: Town of the Dead (2017)

Genres: Horror, Documentary, Drama

Plot: Witness the shocking evidence unfold as paranormal researchers investigate the last remaining building of the ghost town of Iva, Michigan. The old general store, which looms over Dice road, serves as the final monument to those who perished in a deadly plague. When the Midcalf family decided to rescue an old Michigan landmark, they soon realized they were the ones in need of saving.
