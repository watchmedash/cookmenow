Bad Cop (2023)

Genres: Action, Drama, Mystery, Crime

Plot: BAD COP follows the life of a corrupt Policeman, INSPECTOR TAFARI. The story tells of how he aids many criminals in carrying out their criminal activities in his territory and further encourages them by partnering with them for financial gain. He would go as far as burning criminal case files to remain in favour with drug lords. As the story proceeds, TAFARI falls in love with a lady named MIA, who changes his life's trajectory.
