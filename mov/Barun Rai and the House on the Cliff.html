Barun Rai and the House on the Cliff (2021)

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Fantasy, Horror

Plot: Harmesh and Soumili move into their dream home, unbeknownst to the spate of mysterious suicides plaguing the area and local police force. Perplexed and alarmed by the high rate of unexplained deaths, the pair seek answers from Detective Barun Rai, a specialist investigator famed for unravelling paranormal crimes.
