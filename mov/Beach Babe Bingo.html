Beach Babe Bingo (2018)

Genres: Comedy

Plot: Two best friends rent a beach house for the weekend only to discover it’s been double booked so they’ll have to share the place... with two beautiful women! To win the affection of these gorgeous gals our wannabe studs must throw the best beach bash ever to win the legendary Horndogs Beach Party. However a nosey neighbor and some selfish dude-bros have sabotage on their minds. Will our heroes win the girls' hearts? Can this be the greatest beach party ever? What time is the wet t-shirt contest? All will be revealed. Don’t forget the lotion... the sun screen, you perverts.
