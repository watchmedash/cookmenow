Bikini Summer (1991)

Genres: Comedy

Plot: Meet photographer, Chet. He's obsessed with finding "Miss Right." And it looks like Jazz will fit the bill. After all, she has all the right parts, in all the right places, and it's all the right size! (Plus, she was the "body double" for Julie Roberts in Pretty Woman!) She's the leader of an unemployed all - female band. And her buddy Mad Dog knows a perfect way to help Jazz out - he'll put on a bikini contest that will draw everyone's attention. Everyone - including the cops! And when Chet, Jazz and Mad Dog end up as roommates in a beach house, they're in for a summer they'll never forget. And neither will you!
