Death Down the Aisle (2024)

Genres: Thriller, Drama, Crime, TV Movie

Plot: Malorie is torn apart to see her fiancé die on their wedding day before they can say “I do”. The autopsy report shows foul play, and she no longer knows who she can trust. After someone else who is close to Malorie is killed, she begins to fear that the murderer is closer to her than ever and she is left to wonder if she could be the next target.
