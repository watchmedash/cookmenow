Dr. Goldfoot and the Girl Bombs (1966)

Genres: Science Fiction, Comedy

Plot: The scheming mad scientist Dr. Goldfoot (Vincent Price) plots another mad scheme to take over the world by killing off the major military leaders of every country; to that end, he creates in his secret lab a bevy of bodacious girl bombs; full-length, life-size robots that explode when embraced.
