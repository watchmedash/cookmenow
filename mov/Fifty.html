Fifty (1999)

Genres: Documentary

Plot: Prepare yourself for the jaw-dropping highlights from an amazing 50 years of winter sport action and adventure! Join an international array of Olympic skiers and snowboarders as they challenge the steepest slopes and most exciting conditions in exotic snow-covered settings around the globe! It’s the ultimate world tour for anyone who craves thrill-packed entertainment in the extreme! Warren Miller’s Fifty celebrate an incredible half-century of bringing you the hottest cold-weather action ever captured on film!
