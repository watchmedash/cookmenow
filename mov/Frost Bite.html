Frost Bite (2011)

Genres: Action, Horror

Plot: After losing her son in the massive zombie attack Reagan is forced to walk the back woods of frozen Alaska. While doing so she meets Colby Jack and Chris in their custom Winnebago. She is safe for now. Taken back to a small store dubbed "Frost Bite" she will help the remaining survivors fight not only zombies but a religious mad man known as Regis. But all is not lost with leadership of ex-marine John Bannister they just might make it to see another day. Written by Pop Gun Pictures
