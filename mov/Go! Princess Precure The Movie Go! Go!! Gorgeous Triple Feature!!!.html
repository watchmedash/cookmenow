Go! Princess Precure The Movie: Go! Go!! Gorgeous Triple Feature!!! (2015)

Genres: Animation, Fantasy, Comedy, Action, Adventure, Family

Plot: Go! Princess Pretty Cure: Go! Go!! Gorgeous Triple Feature!!! is the 12th Pretty Cure movie produced by Toei Animation. The movie was released in Japanese cinemas on October 31, 2015. It consists of 3 features; A Precious Treasure of Pumpkin Land, Lefy's Wonderful Night, and Cure Flora and A Mysterious Mirror.
