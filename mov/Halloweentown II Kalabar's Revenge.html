Halloweentown II: Kalabar's Revenge (2001)

Genres: Adventure, Family, Fantasy, TV Movie

Plot: The Cromwell clan lives in the real world, except for their grandmother who lives in Halloweentown, a place where monsters go to escape reality. But now the son of the Cromwells' old enemy Kalabar has a plan to use the grandmother's book to turn Halloweentown into a grey dreary version of the real world while transforming the denizens of the real world into monsters.
