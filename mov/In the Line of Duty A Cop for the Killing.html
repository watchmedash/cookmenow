In the Line of Duty: A Cop for the Killing (1990)

Genres: Action, Crime, Drama, Thriller, TV Movie

Plot: A look at the dangerous and bizarre life of an undercover cop who lives on the edge and the strife and heartbreak that comes with the job. There is also a tremendous amount of guilt and psychological pain when an undercover sting goes bad and they lose a comrade in the line of duty.
