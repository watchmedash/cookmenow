Kidnapper (2013)

Genres: Comedy

Plot: In today's metropolitan life, with the rise of nuclear family, a child grows slowly but learns quickly. He learns that he has to study, build his career and survive in this competitive world. His parents makes him learn that he has to win the rat race but fails to teach that even by winning the race, he will still remain a rat. A child grows without love, attention and company of his parents as they are busy making money. They substitute love with computer and video games. But deep inside we all know that a child needs his share of love, care and attention for his complete development. And this is his basic right.
