Kunal Rao: Done (2019)

Genres: Comedy

Plot: Stand up comedian Kunal Rao narrates his personal experiences from the point of view of a cynical, awkward, introverted human being stuck in a world filled with humans who are chasing all the wrong things. And yet, there is a warmth and hopefulness in his voice, despite having no idea what he’s doing with his life.
