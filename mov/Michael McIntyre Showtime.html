Michael McIntyre: Showtime (2012)

Genres: Comedy

Plot: Showtime is Michael McIntyre at his breathtaking best. Following his two record-breaking DVDs, Live and Laughing and Hello Wembley, Michael returns with his most hilarious show yet. Recorded during his phenomenal 10 night run at The 02 in London and part of his 71-date arena tour playing to over 700,000 fans. Michael raises the bar once again with his relentless energy and razor-sharp observations that has the crowd roaring with laughter!
