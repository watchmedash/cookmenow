Playboy Video Centerfold: Carmella DeCesare - Playmate of the Year 2004 (2004)

Genres: Documentary

Plot: A sexy Italian/Puerto Rican model and college senior from Cleveland, Carmella first caught Playboy's eye as one of 12 finalists for the FOX-TV reality show Girl Next Door: The Search for a Playboy Centerfold. Although she dropped out of the competition early, Hugh Hefner recognized her potential and invited her to the Playboy Mansion. With the support of her mom, who auditioned for Playboy 20 years earlier. Carmella seized the opportunity to do a test shoot and her Playboy career skyrocketed from there - first as Playboy.com's February 2003 Cyber Girl of the Month, then PLAYBOY magazine's Miss April 2003, and ultimately our Playmate of the Year 2004! Carmella's natural radiance lights up the screen in this sensual look at our girl-next-door beauty. Discover Carmella's true charms as she shares a cab or the ride of a lifetime, then visits an Italian villa for a voyeuristic fantasy, and unleashes her most secret desires in a steamy dream sequence... and much, much more!
