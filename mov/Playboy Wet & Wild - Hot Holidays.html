Playboy: Wet & Wild - Hot Holidays (1995)

Genres:

Plot: Grab your travel bag and bead for excitement, as the world’s most beautiful women take you on the vacation of a lifetime. They’re wet, wild and on the move and making a splash as Playboy’s breathtaking Playmates show you erotic escapades from exotic destinations. You’ll go skinny-dipping in a tranquil lagoon, take the plunge in a steamy shower of seduction, enjoy a flirty and fabulous fashion show in and out of vacation wear, and that’s just for starters. You’ll venture to an enchanted island off Bora Bora where a lovely marooned damsel awaits your rescue and passionate seduction. Next, three beauties working 9 to 5 take you on a Hawaiian getaway where you’ll discover the deeply provocative pleasures of the tropics, and surf’s up for a fun-loving trio as they catch a wave to sensual surrender. Then, a French frolic recalls the hey-day of 1940’s swing with dancing in the streets and a wet and wild celebration that bares all, and so much more.
