Razor Sharpe (2001)

Genres: Action

Plot: With dreams of becoming the next big action film hero, struggling actor Razor Sharpe (director Troy N. Ashford) takes a bit part in a movie with a bank robbery scene, only to discover that the heist is real -- and that he's been set up to be the fall guy. Now wanted by the law, the hunky hero sets out on a journey filled with nonstop martial arts mayhem to find the crooks who framed him. Michael Smalls and Kevin Kilgore co-star.
