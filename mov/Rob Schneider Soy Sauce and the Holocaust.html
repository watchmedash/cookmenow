Rob Schneider: Soy Sauce and the Holocaust (2013)

Genres: Comedy

Plot: "SNL" alum Rob Schneider takes to the stage (as himself) to examine the bizarre nature of fame, the woes of aging and the virtue of sweatpants. Known for his off-the-wall characters, Schneider opens up about his own life, which can be equally zany.
