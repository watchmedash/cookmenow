Shopkins World Vacation (2017)

Genres: Animation, Family

Plot: Grab your boarding pass and come on a worldwide ride with our favorite Shoppies and Shopkins Vacation with the gang as they meet crazy bandits and go on even crazier adventures! When Kooky Cookie gets caught up in a diamond heist and goes missing, the Shoppies must go on a wild chase around the world to find her and help bring back the 'Shop Diamond' to its royal owner. Watch as they attempt amazing challenges and face Kooky fans o try and clear her name. Fasten your seatbelt for a world vacation you'll never forget.
