Sin Eater (2022)

Genres: Horror, Thriller, Mystery

Plot: A young woman on the run from her shadowy past suffers a terrifying injury that strands in her in a lonely, out of the way town with a dark secret. As she tries to recuperate bizarre phenomena start occurring and growing in intensity. With no one to trust she will have to face not only her inner demons, her checkered past but actual demons who want to take her soul!
