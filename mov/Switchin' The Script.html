Switchin' The Script (2012)

Genres: Comedy

Plot: During a weekend corporate retreat, her best friends convince unlucky-in-love Lonnie to "switch the script" and take control of men. Lonnie impresses every man in the place with her beauty and charm-most importantly, her boss' good-looking son David. Now, Lonnie tries to embrace her new personality without turning away what quite possibly may be "Mr. Right."
