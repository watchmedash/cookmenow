The Five People You Meet In Heaven (2004)

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Science Fiction, TV Movie

Plot: On his 83rd birthday, Eddie, a war vet and a maintenance worker at the Ruby Pier amusement park, dies while trying to save a girl who is sitting under a falling ride. When he awakens in the afterlife, he encounters five people with ties to his corporeal existence who help him understand the meaning of his life.
