Genres: Action

In Mu Suong village, he not only faces Ba Rau - his wife's rival - but also discovers a series of terrifying secrets here. Ba Rau is also the long-term fear of the people here. Fate once again gives Phil in the middle of a choice: vengeance on Ba Rau or protect the people of Mu Suong village?
