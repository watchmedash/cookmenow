The Iceman Tapes: Conversations with a Killer (1992)

Genres: Documentary, Crime, Thriller, Horror, TV Movie

Plot: Richard Kuklinski was a devoted husband, loving father--and ruthless killer of over 100 people. You'll meet him in this powerful documentary that features one of the most vivid and disturbing interviews ever recorded--taped behind the walls of the prison where Kuklinski is serving two consecutive life sentences for multiple homicide.
