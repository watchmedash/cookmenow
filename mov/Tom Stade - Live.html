Tom Stade - Live (2013)

Genres: Comedy

Plot: Star of Live At The Apollo, Michael McIntyre’s Comedy Roadshow, Dave’s One Night Stand and Channel 4’s Stand-Up For The Week. Don’t miss the hugely talented Canadian exile Tom Stade’s debut DVD. His magnetic stage presence, irrepressible charm and first-class story-telling have established him as one of the most sought after performers on the UK and international circuit. Join Tom’s offbeat world as he recounts his global adventures; with a little help from inimitable sidekick Jimmy.
