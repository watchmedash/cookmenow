X Trillion (2024)

Genres: Documentary

Plot: A journey that promises to change not only the women's lives but the very future of our planet. X Trillion follows fourteen women as they sail a gruelling 3000 miles across the North Pacific Ocean to one of the most remote places on Earth, but also the location of the densest accumulation of ocean plastic – the Great Pacific Garbage Patch.​ A life-afﬁrming, optimistic story of adventure, discovery, science and solidarity.
