Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/3 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 sliced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it will go. If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.
  2. Roll out your pizza dough to your desired thickness. If you're using a pizza stone, place the dough on a piece of parchment paper.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella over the sauce. Arrange the tomato slices on top of the cheese.
  5. Transfer the pizza (with the parchment paper if using a pizza stone) to the oven. Bake until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and starting to brown, about 10-15 minutes.
  6. Remove the pizza from the oven. Scatter the fresh basil leaves over the top. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Time

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
